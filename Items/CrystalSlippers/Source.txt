#### Crystal Slippers
*Wondrous item, uncommon (requires attunement)*

This footwear is made from an enchanted crystalline material with a glassy finish.  While you are wearing both these slippers, when you take the disengage action you may also dash as a bonus action.

Additionally, you can retain your attunement to the slippers even if you lose one of them, but both slippers are required for the initial attunement. 

A creature that is holding the lost slipper knows your location as if they cast the *Locate Creature* spell on you.
