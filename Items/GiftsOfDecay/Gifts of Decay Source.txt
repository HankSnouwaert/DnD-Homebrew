# Gifts of Plague
Powerful demons or barbarian tribes of war and rage will sometimes craft magical items which channel their bloodthirsty powers.  They may wield these items themselves or gift them to worthy warriors.  The following magic items can be used to represent these gifts of excess in your campaign.

#### Flail of Pestilence (24,000g)
*Flail, very rare*<br> (requires attunement)

Said to be crafted from growths and encrusted puss of a demon lore of pestilence. It inflicts demonic rot onto thouse that it strikes 

Once during your turn, when you hit a creature with an attack using this Magic Weapon, the target must make a DC 17 Constitution saving throw or take 3d8 necrotic damage. After failing three of these Saving Throws with in one minute, the creature is under the disease's effects from contagion spell for one minute.

#### Flesh Peeler (26000g)
*Wondrous item, very rare*<br> (Requires Attunement by a Druid, Sorcerer, Warlock, or Wizard)

"When this cursed bell rings, necrotic waves spills forth form it escalate the uses necrotic magic to case the flesh blacken and bled  ""
Once per turn while you are holding the bell, when you hit a creature with a spell attack that deals necrotic damage or a creatures fails saving throw against that deals necrotic damage, you can wound the target. At the start of each of the wounded creature’s turns, it takes 1d4 necrotic damage for each time you’ve wounded it, and it can then make a DC 15 Constitution saving throw, Ending the Effect of all such wounds on itself on a success. Alternatively, the wounded creature, or a creature within 5 feet of it, can use an action to make a DC 15 Wisdom (Medicine) check, Ending the Effect of such wounds on it on a success.

#### Foetid Shroud (6000g) 
*Wondrous item, very rare*<br> (requires attunement)

The shroud feels and smells as though it has been dossed in fever-sweat, the air around the shroud is thick and chocks any who linger in around it

While creature is wearing this Shroud, Any creature other then wear that starts its turn within 5 feet of it must succeed on a DC 13 Constitution saving throw or be poisoned until the start of the creature's next turn.

A creature that successfully saves against this effect is immune to the effects of this Shroud's stench for 1 hour.

#### Helm of the Blighter (7000)
*rare* (requires attunement by a druid)

Made of rotting animal bones with moss and blight covering it, The Helm is worn by thoughs that make mockery of the natural oder.

When wear would transform form wild shape, they may choose to take necrotic damage equal to 5 x CR of the creature you are transforming into. This damage can't be reduced or prevented in any way and you hit point maximum is reduced by an amount equal to the damage taken. This reduction lasts until you finishes a Long Rest. The you dies if this effect reduces your hit point maximum to 0.

If you do this you wild shape gains the fallowing changes.

* Your creature type counts as undead.
* You are immune to necrotic and poison damage and the poison condition.
* Whenever you deal damage with a attack in beast form, you can change the damage type to necrotic or poison damage.
* Your beast from hit points are increase by the amount equal to the necrotic damage you took from the Helm.


#### Rot Blade  (1,000g)
*Weapon (any sword), very rare*<br> (requires attunement)
The blade is covered in rust and encrusted guts of plague victims, those that are cut with the blade quickly become infected with lethal diseases.

Once per turn, when you hit a creature with an Attack using this Magic Weapon, It must make a DC 14 Constitution saving throw or be inflicted with disease,  At the start of each of the inflicted creature’s turns, it takes 1d6 necrotic damage, and it can then make a DC 14 Constitution saving throw, Ending the Effect. Onces a creature becomes infected by the blade, it can't be infected again for the next 24 hours

#### Shield of Growths  (3,000g)
*Wondrous item, legendary*<br>

The rusted frame is covered in living blubber and grim which feeds on the beara own wounds to grow onto the frame stronger.

While holding this shield, when ever you take damage form a attack, the shield gets +1 bonus to AC until the start of your next turn, it can gain a max of +4.

#### Staff of Decay (26000g) 
*Wondrous item, very rare*<br> (Requires Attunement by a Druid, Sorcerer, Warlock, or Wizard)

Craved From the rooting wood of a treant, the staff holds the power of rot with it's core.

You have Resistance to necrotic damage while you hold this staff.

The staff has 10 Charges. While holding it, you can use an action to expend 1 or more of its Charges to cast one of the following Spells from it, using your spell save DC: Circle of Death (6 charges), Inflict Wounds (1 charge), Stream of Corruption (2 charges), or Blight (4 charges).

The staff regains 1d6 + 4 expended Charges daily at dawn. If you expend the last charge, roll a d20. On a 1. the staff turns to water and is destroyed.
