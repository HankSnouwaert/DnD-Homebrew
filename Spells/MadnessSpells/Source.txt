## Madness Spells

Some spell test the limits of the caster that wield it, driving them to insanity. Spells that have a **Madess** tag means when a creature cast the spell it can chose to gain the benefits under the tag, if it does, when all the effects of the spell end, the creature must roll on the Madness Table and suffer the effect rolled.

A Creature can cure it's self of **one** madness effects over a long rest. it can attempt to cure each other maddness effect with a Wisdom or Charisma (It's choice) the dc is equal to the spell dc of the spell that cased the madness

A Calm Emotions spell can suppress the Effects of madness, Remove Curse ,Greater Restoration or dispel evil will remove a madness effect.

#### Creeping Smite
*1st-level evocation*
___
- **Casting Time:** 1 bonus action
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V
- **Duration:**  Concentration, up to 1 minute
- **Classes:**  Paladin
___
The next time you hit a creature with a melee weapon attack during the spell’s duration, your weapon is swathed in purple light, your attack deals an extra 2d4 psychic damage. if the attacked creature has no allies within 5 feet of it, you deal a extra 3d4 psychic damage.

**At Higher Levels.** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2rd level or higher, the initial and the extra damage from a creature that has no has no allies increases by 1d4 for each slot level above 2nd.

**Madness** - The duration of the spell becomes 10 minutes, you become invisible and don't gain disadvantage form Armor you are wearing on Dexterity (Stealth) checks until the spell ends or until a you make a damage roll, or force someone to make a saving throw. The spells continues even when invisibility ends.

#### Fiery Temper
*1st-level evocation*
___
- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:**  100 feet
- **Components:**  V S
- **Duration:**  Instantaneous
- **Classes:**  Cleric, Sorcerer, Warlock
___
Vengeful flames spring out and strike toward a creature of your choice within range. Make a ranged spell attack against the target. On a hit, the target takes 4d6 fire damage. when you make the attack, you can decide gain advantage on the attack roll, but Attack rolls against you have advantage until your next turn.

**At Higher Levels:** When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 1st.

**Madness** - Dices for the damage rolls on the spell Change to d12 instead of d6.
