#### The Celestial Invocations.

#### Sunsteel
Prerequisite: The Celestial patron, Pact of the Blade feature. 


When you create your pact weapon. You can choose have it be made into a spear of gleaming gold, Your attacks with the spear ignore half and three-quarters cover, aslo once during your turn when you hit a creature with the spear, you can choose to cast Sacred Flame without components on the creature instead of dealing the weapons damage. the creature automatically fails the saving throw.

#### Sigil of Protection 
Prerequisite: The celestial patron, Pact of the Talisman feature

When you use you Healing Light feature, you may teleport and place Talisman on the healed creature, Until the end of your next turn, that creature gains bonus to it's AC equal the number dice spent on the feature as long as it is wearing the talisman.


#### Scroll of the Divine

Prerequisite: The Celestial patron, Pact of the tome feature.


Choose one spells from the clerics spell list of second or first level. You can cast the chosen spell with out expending a spell slot and it counts as warlock spell for you. Once you do so, you can't cast it in this way again until you finish a long rest.

#### Guiding Herald
Prerequisite: The Celestial patron, Pact of the chain feature.

When you use you Healing Light feature, your familiar can its reaction to take the help action if it is with in 5 feet of the creature healed this way.


#### Sigil of Light
Prerequisite: The celestial patron, Pact of the Talisman feature

You learn the Warding Bond spell witch counts as a Warlock spell for you, the material competent for the spell is your Talisman which target must have for the duration
