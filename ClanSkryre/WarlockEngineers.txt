> ## Ratfolk Warlock Engineer
>*Medium humanoid (ratfolk), chaotic evil*
> ___
> - **Armor Class** 15 (integrated armor)
> - **Hit Points** 120 (16d8 + 48)
> - **Speed** 30 ft.
>___
>|STR|DEX|CON|INT|WIS|CHA|
>|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|
>|16 (+3)|12 (+1)|16 (+3)|16 (+3)|12 (+1)|14 (+2)|
>___
> - **Saving Throws** Con +6, Int +6
> - **Damage Resistances** one damage type of its choice other than bludgeoning, piercing, or slashing (It can change the damage type after a long rest if it has Smith's Tools on hand.)
> - **Skills** Arcana +6, Deception +5, Land Vehicles +6, Smith's Tools +6, Tinker's Tools +6
> - **Senses** darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 11
> - **Languages** Common, Undercommon
> - **Challenge** 5 (1,800 XP) &emsp;&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;    **Proficiency Bonus** +3
> ___
>
>
> ***Crevice Crawler.*** The ratfolk can move through and stop in a space large enough to fit a creature one size smaller than it without squeezing.
>
> ***Devious Engineer.*** The ratfolk can use its actions to reload a firearm, or make a check using a tool while frightened by its Scurry Away feature. It also does not gain disadvantage on tool checks from being frightened.
>
> ***Forbidden Devices.*** The ratfolk has 2 Forbidden Devices listed below. The DC for any saves made by devices is 17.
>
> ***Ingenious Weaponry.*** The ratfolk may use its Intelligence modifier instead of its Dexterity or Strength modifier for attack and damage rolls.
>
> ***Keen Smell.*** The ratfolk has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on smell.
>
> ***More Power!*** At the start of the ratfolk's turn, if it is not incapacitated, it can accumulate eldrich power. It rolls a 1d4 and gains a number of levels of eldritch power equal to the result for one minute or until it uses this feature again. If it rolls a 4, it takes 18 (4d8) lightning damage, ignoring resistance, and loses all its current power levels.
>
> ***Overwhelming Mass x3.*** The first time each turn that the ratfolk hits a creature with an attack, it deals an extra 3 damage for each ally it has within 5 feet of the target that isn't incapacitated, up to a maximum of 3 allies.
>
> ### Bonus Actions
>
> ***Jerry-rig.*** The ratfolk reloads a firearm or makes a check using a tool.  The ratfolk reloads a firearm or makes a check using a tool. 
>
> ### Actions
>
> ***Multiattack.*** The ratfolk makes two attacks.
>
> ***Warplock Pistols.*** *Ranged Weapon Attack:* +6 to hit, range 60/150 ft., one target. *Hit:* 9 (1d12 + 3) piercing damage plus 7 (2d6) radiant damage. If the ratfolk rolls a 3 or lower on the attack roll, the attack automatically misses as the pistol malfuctions and the ratfolk takes the attack damage. A fired pistol must be reloaded using an action before it can be fired again.
>
> ***Warp Blade.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +6 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. *Hit:* 8 (1d10 + 3) slashing damage, and the ratfolk can choose to expend all of its eldritch power levels to deal an additional 5 (1d10) lightning damage per level expended.
>
>
> ### Reactions
>
> ***Scurry Away.*** When an enemy the ratfolk can see ends its turn within its sight, the ratfolk can move up to half its speed away from the creature and become frightened of it until the end of the creature's next turn. This movement doesn't provoke opportunity attacks. While frightened this way, the ratfolk can only take the Dash, Disengage, Dodge, or Hide action. This feature ignores immunity to the frightened condition.
