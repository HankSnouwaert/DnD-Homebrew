> ## Exalted Bloodthirster
>*Huge fiend (demon), chaotic evil*
> ___
> - **Armor Class** 18 (Plate)
> - **Hit Points** 310 (27d12+135)
> - **Speed** 40ft., fly 50 ft.
>___
>|STR|DEX|CON|INT|WIS|CHA|
>|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|
>|24 (+7)|20 (+5)|20 (+5)|18 (+4)|20 (+5)|20 (+5)|
>___
> - **Saving Throws** Con +10, Dex +10, Int +5, Wis +10,
> - **Skills** Acrobatics +10, Athletics +19, intimidation +11, Perception +11
> - **Damage Resistances** cold, lightning; bludgeoning, 
piercing, and slashing from nonmagical attacks
> - **Damage Immunities** poison, fire
> - **Condition Immunities** charmed, frightened, poisoned
> - **Senses** Blindsight 10 Ft., Truesight 120 ft., Darkvision 300 ft., passive Perception 21
> - **Languages** Abyssal, telepathy 300 ft.
> - **Challenge** 20 (18,000 XP)   &emsp;&emsp; **Proficiency Bonus** +6        
> ___
>
> ***The Land Rebels.*** Bloodthirster ignores difficult terrain, in addition any ground the Bloodthirster moves though becomes difficult terrain, and is set ablaze with fire for the next minute, A non-demon creature takes the 10(3d6) fire when damage it enters the area for the first time on a turn or ends its turn there.
>
> ***Magic Resistance.*** The Bloodthirster has advantage on saving throws 
against spells and other magical effects.
>
> **Legendary Resistance** (3/Day) If the Bloodthirster fails a saving throw, it
can choose to succeed instead.
>
> ***Weapons of Fury.*** Any weapons the Bloodthirster wielding becomes magical for long as it wields it and Bloodthirster can't be disarmed of the weapon.
>
> ***SpellBreaker*** At the start of each of Bloodthirster turns, it can end one spell of 6th level or lower of it's choice effecting it or a creature with in 10 feet of it that it can see. It can do this even while incapacitated.
>___
> ### Bonus Actions
> ***Cleave.***  When the Bloodthirster reduces a creature to 0 hit points with it's Greataxe of Fury on its turn, the Bloodthirster can take a bonus action to Greataxe of Fury.
>
> ***Aggressive.*** The Bloodthirster moves up to its speed toward a hostile creature 
it can see.
>___
> ### Actions
> ***Multiattack.*** The keeper makes three attacks.
>
> ***Lash of Khorne.*** Melee Weapon Attack: +13 to hit, reach 60 ft. , one  target. Hit: 20 (3d8 + 7) slashing damage. The target must make a DC 21 strength saving throw or be pulled 30 feet towards the Bloodthirster.
>
> **Greataxe of Khorne.** Melee Weapon Attack: +13 to hit, reach 10ft., one target. Hit: 27 (3d12 + 7) slashing damage. When a creature that is concentrating on a spell is damage by the Greataxe, that creature has disadvantage on the saving throw it makes to maintain its concentration.
>
> **Whirlwind of Rage** the Bloodthirster makes a **Greataxe of Fury.** attack against any number of creatures within 10 feet of it, with a separate attack roll for each target. The area with in 10 feet of the Bloodthirster is also effected by the ***The Land Rebels.*** feature above.
>
> ### Legendary Actions
> The stallion can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action option can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn. The Bloodthirster regains spent legendary actions at the start of its turn.
> 
> **Check.** The Bloodthirster makes a skill check that would normally require an action.
> 
> **Move.** The Bloodthirster moves up to half its speed without provoking attacks of opportunity.
>
> **Whip.** The Bloodthirster makes a Lash of Khorne attack.
> 
> **Greataxe. (Costs 2 Actions)** The Bloodthirster makes a Greataxe of Khorne attack.
>
> **Rejoice in the Slaughter (Costs 3 Actions)**  Each creature of the Bloodthirster's choice that is within 120 feet of it, that can hear or see it, gain advantage on their next attack roll until the end of the Bloodthirster's next turn. If the attack hits it is a critical hit if the attacker is within 10 feet of the creature.
> 
> **Reconstitute (Costs 3 Actions).** If the Bloodthirster is suffering from one or more conditions that require it to make a saving throw to end, it may immediately make a save against each condition. A successful save for any of the throws will end the condition immediately. The only saving throws that this action can be used for are saves that are made at the end/start of its next turn or using its action. It can use this action while incapacitated.
>___
> ### Reactions
> ***Wrathful Charge.*** When the Bloodthirster is hit by a attack or a creature cast a spell that forces the Bloodthirster to make a saving throw to resist its effects, the Bloodthirster can move up to half it speed towards the creature without provoking opportunity attacks, the Bloodthirster can then make a melee weapon attack against that creature with advantage if it is with in 10 feet of it.

> ## Exalted Lord of Change
>*Huge fiend (demon), chaotic evil*
> ___
> - **Armor Class** 16 (natural armor)
> - **Hit Points** 283 (27d12+108)
> - **Speed** 40ft. fly 80 ft.
>___
>|STR|DEX|CON|INT|WIS|CHA|
>|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|
>|18 (+4)|18 (+4)|18 (+4)|25 (+7)|22 (+6)|22 (+6)|
>___
> - **Saving Throws** Con +10, CHA +12, Wis +12, Int +13
> - **Skills** Arcana +19, History +19 Deception +10, Insight +10, Perception +12
> - **Damage Resistances** cold, lightning; bludgeoning, 
piercing, and slashing from nonmagical attacks
> - **Damage Immunities** poison, fire
> - **Condition Immunities** poisoned
> - **Senses** truesight 120 ft., darkvision 300 ft., passive Perception 22
> - **Languages**  all, telepathy 300 ft.
> - **Challenge** 17 (18,000 XP)   &emsp;&emsp; **Proficiency Bonus** +6        
> ___
>
> ***Distant Caster.*** When Lord of Change cast a spell that has a range of 5 feet or greater, double the range of the spell.
 When Lord of Change cast a spell that has a range of touch, The range of the spell 30 feet.
>
> ***Improved Spell Casting.*** When Lord of Change cast an spell that requires it to make an ability check as a part of casting that spell (as in Counterspell, Dispel Magic, Chaos Strom), it ads it's proficiency bonus to that ability check.
>
> **Resistance Shredder** Lord of Change's spells it casts ignore resistance. 
>
> ***Warp Flame.*** When a creature is reduced to 0 hit points by of the Lord of Change's spells, that creature must make a DC 21 Charisma saving throw at the a end of it's next turn if it is still has 0 hit points, if the target fails it dies and instantly transforms into a living Pink Horror of Tzeentch under the Lord of Change's control. The transformation of the body can be undone only by a wish spell.
>
> ***Magic Resistance.*** The Lord of Change has advantage on saving throws 
against spells and other magical effects.
>
> **Legendary Resistance** (3/Day) If the Lord of Change fails a saving throw, it
can choose to succeed instead.
>
> ***Dual Minded*** If the Lord of Change attempts to cast a spell that requires concentration while already concentrating on an existing spell, it can maintain concentration on both spells simultaneously. if it needs to make a Concentration saving throw to maintain concentration on both spells, it makes the save with advantage. On a failure it loses concentration on one of the spells. (it's choice)
>
> ### Actions
> ***Multiattack.*** The keeper makes three attacks: one with its 
Baleful Sword, one with it's staff, one with it's beak, Alternatively, it can replace any of it's attacks to cast a at will spell.
>
>
> ***Staff of Tzeentch.*** Melee Weapon Attack: +10 to hit, reach 10 ft. , one target. Hit: 19 (4d6 + 4) bludgeoning damage and if the target is a creature, it has disadvantage on it's next saving throw until the end of the Lord of Change's next turn.
>
> ***Beak.*** Melee Weapon Attack: +10 to hit, reach 10 ft. , one target. Hit: 19 (4d6 + 5) piercing damage.
>
> ***Baleful Sword.*** Melee Weapon Attack: +10 to hit, reach 10 ft. , one  target. Hit: 17 (3d8 + 4) slashing damage plus 13 (3d8) fire damage.
>
> ***Spellcasting.*** The keeper casts one of the following spells, requiring no components and using intelligence as its spellcasting ability (spell save DC 21):
>
>***At will:*** *Burning hands (5th level)*, *Dissonant Whispers (6th level)* *Aganazzar's Scorcher (4th level)* *Flaming Sphere (4th level)* **Glean Magic (7th level) *, *Dispel magic* *Nystul's Magic Aura(self only)*, *Invisibility*, *Banishment* *Bolt of Change*
>
> 3/day each: *Counter Spell* *Shield* *Dimension Door* *Arcane Eye*
>
>***1/day each:***  *Fire Storm* *Mind Blank* *Prismatic Spray* *Chaos Strom*,  
>
> ### Bonus Actions
> ***Dimensional Shift.***   The Lord of Change teleports up to 30 feet to an unoccupied space that it can see.
> 
> ### Legendary Actions
> The Lord of Change can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action option can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn. The Lord of Change regains spent legendary actions at the start of its turn.
> 
> **Check.** The Lord of Change makes a skill check that would normally require an action.
> 
> **Move.** The Lord of Change moves up to half its speed without provoking attacks of opportunity.
>
> **Staff.** The Lord of Change makes a Staff of Tzeentch attack.
> 
> **Sword. (Costs 2 Actions)** The Lord of Change makes a Baleful Sword attack.
>
> **Beacon of Sorcery (Costs 3 Actions)**  Each creature of the Exalted Lord of Change's choice that is within 120 feet of it, that can hear or see it, they may cast a spell 4th level or lower on their next turn that has a casting time of 1 action as a bonus action.
> 
> **Reconstitute (Costs 3 Actions).** If the Lord of Change is suffering from one or more conditions that require it to make a saving throw to end, it may immediately make a save against each condition. A successful save for any of the throws will end the condition immediately. The only saving throws that this action can be used for are saves that are made at the end/start of its next turn or using its action. It can use this action while incapacitated.
>
