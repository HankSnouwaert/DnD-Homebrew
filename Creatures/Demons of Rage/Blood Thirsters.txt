> ## Blood Thirster
>*Huge fiend (demon), chaotic evil*
> ___
> - **Armor Class** 18 (plate)
> - **Hit Points** 287 (25d12 + 125)
> - **Speed** 40 ft., fly 50 ft.
>___
>|STR|DEX|CON|INT|WIS|CHA|
>|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|
>|24 (+7)|18 (+4)|20 (+5)|16 (+3)|18 (+4)|18 (+4)|
>___
> - **Saving Throws** Dex +10, Con +11, Wis +10, Int +9
> - **Skills** Acrobatics +10, Athletics +19, Intimidation +16, Perception +10
> - **Damage Resistances** cold, lightning; bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing from nonmagical attacks
> - **Damage Immunities** fire, poison
> - **Condition Immunities** charmed, frightened, poisoned
> - **Senses** blindsight 10 ft., darkvision 300 ft., truesight 120 ft., passive Perception 20
> - **Languages** Abyssal, Telepathy 300 ft.
> - **Challenge** 17 (18,000 XP) &emsp;&emsp;&ensp;&nbsp; **Proficiency Bonus** +6        
> ___
> ***Magic Resistance.*** The blood thirster has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.
>
> ***Spell Breaker.*** At the start of each of the blood thirster's turns, it can end one spell of 3rd level or lower affecting it or a creature within 10 feet of it that it can see.
>
> ***The Land Rebels.*** The blood thirster ignores difficult terrain. At the end of the blood thirster's turn, any ground within 10 feet of it becomes difficult terrain and is set ablaze for the next minute. Any non-demon of rage creature that ends its turn there takes 13 (4d6) fire damage.
>
> ***Weapons of Fury.*** Weapons the blood thirster wields become magical, and it cannot be disarmed of them.
>
> ### Actions
>
> ***Multiattack.*** The blood thirster makes two attacks, each with a different weapon.
>
> ***Greataxe of Fury.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +13 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. *Hit:* 23 (3d10 + 7) slashing damage. If the target is a creature, it must succeed on a DC 21 Strength saving throw or take 13 (3d8) force damage. If it fails by 10 or more, it takes 27 (6d8) force damage instead. A creature reduced to 0 hit points by this force damage dies.
>
> ***War Whip.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +13 to hit, reach 60 ft., one target. *Hit:* 20 (3d8 + 7) slashing damage. The target must make a DC 21 Strength saving throw or be pulled 30 feet toward the blood thirster. If the creature fails the save by 10 or more, it is knocked prone.
>
> ### Bonus Actions
>
> ***Aggressive.*** The blood thirster can move up to its speed toward a hostile creature that it can see.
>
> ### Reactions
>
> ***Vengeful Strike.*** When hit by an attack, the blood thirster can move up to half its speed towards the attacker without provoking opportunity attacks and make a melee weapon attack if the attacker is within reach.
>
> ***Wrathful Counter.*** If a creature within 120 feet casts a spell, the blood thirster can force the caster to make a DC Constitution saving throw. On a failure, the creature takes 4 (1d8) fire damage per level of the spell being cast. If the creature fails the save by 5 or more, the spell is countered.

> - **Skills** Acrobatics +10, Athletics +19, Intimidation +16, Perception +10
> - **Damage Resistances** cold, lightning; bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing from nonmagical attacks
> - **Damage Immunities** fire, poison
> - **Condition Immunities** charmed, frightened, poisoned

> ___
> ***Magic Resistance.*** The blood thirster has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.
>
> ***The Land Rebels.*** The blood thirster ignores difficult terrain. At the end of its turn, any ground within 10 feet of it becomes difficult terrain and is set ablaze with fire for the next minute. Any non-demon of rage creature that ends its turn there takes 13 (4d6) fire damage.

> ***Spell Breaker.*** At the start of each of its turns, the blood thirster can end one spell of 3rd level or lower affecting it or a creature within 10 feet of it that it can see.

If the target is a creature, it must succeed on a DC 21 Strength saving throw or take 13 (3d8) force damage. If it fails by 10 or more, it takes 27 (6d8) force damage instead. A creature reduced to 0 hit points by this force damage dies.
>
> ***War Whip.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +13 to hit, reach 60 ft., one target. *Hit:* 23 (3d10 + 7) slashing damage. The target must make a DC 21 Strength saving throw or be pulled 30 feet toward the blood thirster. If the creature fails the save by 10 or more, it is knocked prone.
> 
> ### Bonus Actions
> ***Aggressive.*** The blood thirster can move up to its speed toward a hostile creature that it can see.
>

> ***Vengeful Strike.*** When hit by an attack, the Exalted Blood Thirster can move up to half its speed towards the attacker without provoking opportunity attacks and make a melee weapon attack against that creature if within reach.
>
> ***Wrathful Counter.*** When a creature within 120 feet of it casts a spell, the Exalted Blood Thirster can force the caster to make a DC Constitution saving throw equal to 10 + the spell's level. On a failed save, the caster takes 4 (1d8) fire damage per spell level, and the spell has no effect.
> 
> ### Legendary Actions
> The blood thirster can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action option can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn. The blood thirster regains spent legendary actions at the start of its turn.
>
> **Check.** The blood thirster makes a skill check.
>
> **Head Bash.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +13 to hit, reach 5 ft., one creature. *Hit:* The target must succeed on a DC 21 Constitution saving throw or take 16 (3d10) bludgeoning damage, or half as much on a success. If the save fails by 10 or more, the target is dazed until the end of its next turn.
>
> **Move.** The blood thirster moves up to 30 feet without provoking opportunity attacks.
>
> **Tenacious Will.** If the blood thirster is suffering from an effect that allows it to make a saving throw as an action or at the beginning or end of its next turn, it may immediately make that save against the effect with advantage. It can use this legendary action while incapacitated.
>
> **Hellfire Breath (Costs 2 Actions).** Exhales fire in a 60-foot cone. Each creature in the area must make a DC 21 Dexterity saving throw, taking 16 (3d10) fire damage on a failed save, or half as much on a successful one.

> ## The Bloody Fist
> *Huge fiend (demon), chaotic evil*
> ___
> - **Armor Class** 18 (plate)
> - **Hit Points** 345 (30d12 + 150)
> - **Speed** 40 ft., fly 50 ft.
> ___
> |STR|DEX|CON|INT|WIS|CHA|
> |:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|
> |24 (+7)|18 (+4)|20 (+5)|16 (+3)|18 (+4)|18 (+4)|
> ___
> - **Saving Throws** Dex +10, Con +11, Wis +10, Int +9
> - **Skills** Acrobatics +10, Athletics +19, Intimidation +16, Perception +10
> - **Damage Resistances** cold, lightning; bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing from nonmagical attacks
> - **Damage Immunities** fire, poison
> - **Condition Immunities** charmed, frightened, poisoned
> - **Senses** blindsight 10 ft., darkvision 300 ft., truesight 120 ft., passive Perception 20
> - **Languages** Abyssal, telepathy 300 ft.
> - **Challenge** 22 (41,000 XP) &emsp;&emsp;&ensp;&nbsp; **Proficiency Bonus** +6
> ___
>
> ***Blood Spikes.*** The Bloody Fist ignores difficult terrain. At the end of its turn, any ground within 5 feet of it twists and sprouts hard metal spikes, becoming difficult terrain for the next minute. Creatures other than The Bloody Fist moving into or within the area receive a wound unless resistant or immune to piercing damage.
>
> ***Bloody Wounds.*** The Bloody Fist has various abilities that can inflict creatures with wounds. At the end of each of its turns, a wounded creature takes 2 (1d4) necrotic damage for each wound it has sustained. Wounds can be removed the following ways:
> * When a creature is healed, one wound is removed for every 5 hit points of healing it receives. 
> * A creature can expend one use of a healer's kit to remove two wounds from itself or another creature. 
> * A wounded creature, or another creature within 5 feet of it, can make a DC 10 Wisdom (Medicine) check as an action to remove one wound on a success. For every 5 points by which the check exceeds the DC, an additional wound is removed.
> ####
> ***Magic Resistance.*** The Bloody Fist has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.
>
> ***Spell Breaker.*** At the start of its turn, The Bloody Fist can end one spell of 3rd level or lower affecting it or a creature within 10 feet that it can see.
>
> ***Weapons of Fury.*** Weapons wielded by The Bloody Fist become magical, and it cannot be disarmed of these weapons.
> 
> ### Actions
> ***Multiattack.*** The Bloody Fist makes two attacks.
>
> ***Gore Mace.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +13 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. *Hit:* 23 (3d10 + 7) bludgeoning damage, and the target sustains three wounds.
>
> ***Serrated Whip.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +13 to hit, reach 60 ft., one target. *Hit:* 20 (3d8 + 7) slashing damage, and the target sustains a wound. The target must succeed on a DC 21 Strength saving throw or be pulled 30 feet towards The Bloody Fist. If the creature fails the save by 10 or more, it is knocked prone.
>
> ### Bonus Actions
> ***Aggressive.*** The Bloody Fist can move up to its speed toward a hostile creature that it can see.
>
> ### Reactions
> ***Goreshell.*** When a creature within 120 feet takes damage from its wounds, The Bloody Fist can gain temporary hit points equal to the damage dealt.
> 
> ### Legendary Actions
> The Bloody Fist can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn. It regains spent legendary actions at the start of its turn.
>
> **Check.** The Bloody Fist makes a skill check.
>
> **Move.** The Bloody Fist moves up to 30 feet without provoking opportunity attacks.
>
> **Tenacious Will.** If The Bloody Fist is suffering from an effect that allows it to make a saving throw as an action or at the beginning or end of its next turn, it may immediately make that save against the effect with advantage. It can use this legendary action while incapacitated.
>
> **Bloody Knuckles.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +13 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. *Hit:* 13 (1d12 + 7) bludgeoning damage, and the target sustains two wounds.
>
> **Hemorrhaging Roar (Costs 2 Actions).** The Bloody Fist emits a roar in a 120-foot cone, causing open wounds to gush blood. Each creature in the cone's area takes 2 (1d4) necrotic damage for each wound it has sustained.
>
