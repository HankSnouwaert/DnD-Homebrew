#### Locus of Rage Abilities
If a bloodletter has the Locus of Rage trait, you can give it one of the following abilities:

***Locus of Warding.*** The bloodletter and each demon of rage within 30 feet of it has resistance to damage from spells.
 
 ***Locus of Fury.*** All demons of rage within 30 feet of the bloodletter count as being within 10 feet of each other for the purposes of the Murdurous Tide and Merciless Tide abilities.

***Locus of Wrath.*** The bloodletter and each demon of rage within 30 feet of it is under the effects of the *bless* spell for the next minute if they have less than half their hit points.

> ##### Rage Riders
> If you wish to have any bloodletter you're using ride a controlled mount, you can give it the following trait:<br> ***Rage Rider.*** If the bloodletter has a controlled mount that is also a demon of rage, the mount can take the Attack action once on each of the bloodletter's turns and use its bonus action on its turn. If it takes this action, the mount cannot take Dash, Disengage, and Dodge on that turn.
