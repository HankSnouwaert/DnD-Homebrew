## Ratfolk Blight Monk

Soiled and decaying robes hang from bodies riddled with rashes and boils, their diseased flesh barely clinging to their bones as they clutch weapons encrusted with rust and bile. These initiates of the Great Church of Decay, known as Blight Acolytes, are a truly revolting sight. While many ratfolk follow the Verminous God of Ruin, none are more fanatical in their devotion than the Church of Decay.
They preach with fervor that disease and sickness are divine gifts—blessings meant to be wielded as instruments of conquest, spreading their god’s will and bringing about his eternal reign. Those who join the congregation embrace their afflictions, believing their suffering to be a sacred trial. Yet their lives are painfully short; many perish before completing their initiation, while others succumb within days or months of their ordination.

Regardless of how long they endure, these festering zealots serve as the church’s most fearsome agents. Facing them in battle is a dire risk, for even if victorious, one may still fall victim to the myriad of virulent illnesses they carry. And yet, the afflictions that cripple most creatures seem only to strengthen these deranged followers. Their wasted bodies withstand unnatural punishment, while the fevers that burn in their minds drive them into fanatical rages, overriding the ratfolk’s natural instinct to flee from danger. At the height of their maladies, Blight Monks hurl themselves into battle in spasmodic frenzies—swinging wildly, frothing at the mouth, and howling praise to their loathsome god.

Even among their own kind, the Church of Decay is feared and reviled. To most ratfolk, these disease-ridden fanatics are unhinged, their suicidal devotion beyond comprehension. Yet to the faithful, those who reject the path of decay are weak in their belief. When the time comes, their lord’s blessed sickness will convert them to the fold—or consume them.


Those acolytes who survive the anointing of the Church of Decay’s diseases eventually ascend to the rank of Plague Monk, fully inducted into the order. As they continue their unholy service, they willingly take on even more contagions, as each affliction elevates them to greater heights of fevered zeal.

### Ratfolk Soldiers & Seers Loot
Because ratfolk are humanoids, most of the valuable goods that can be looted from them are the remains of the equipment they were using or valuables they were carrying or hiding nearby. Most of the challenge of looting ratfolk is identifying which materials they're carrying that have functional value.

Finding valuables can be done using an **Intelligence (Investigation)** or **Wisdom (Perception)** check, depending on the circumstance. In the case of a grey prophet, you may use an **Intelligence (Arcana)** or **Intelligence (Religion)** check instead.
