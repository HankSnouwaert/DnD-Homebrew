# Ratfolk Ninja Masters

___
___
> ## Eshin Sorcerer
>*Medium humanoid, unaligned*
> ___
> - **Armor Class** 15 (Studded leather)
> - **Hit Points**  71 (13d8+13)
> - **Speed** 30 ft.
>___
>|STR|DEX|CON|INT|WIS|CHA|
>|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|
>|10 (+0)|14 (+2)|12 (+1)|14 (+2)|16 (+3)|12 (+1)|
>___
> - **Saving Throws**  Dex +5 Int +5, Wis +6
> - **Skills** Arcane + 5, Acrobatics + 4 Stealth + 4, Perception + 6
> - **Senses** darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 16
> - **Languages** Common, Undercommon
> - **Challenge** 6 (25 XP) &emsp;&emsp;&nbsp;    **Proficiency Bonus** +3
> ___
>
>
> ***Unarmored Defense*** While the ratfolk is wearing no armor and wielding no shield, its AC includes its Wisdom modifier.
>
> ***Crevice Crawler.*** The ratfolk can move through and stop in a space large enough to fit a creature one size smaller than it without squeezing.
>
> ***Keen Smell.*** The ratfolk has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on smell.
>
> ***Overwhelming Mass (3).*** The first time each turn that the ratfolk hits a creature with an attack, it deals an extra 3 damage per each ally within 5 feet of the target that isn't incapacitated, up to a maximum of 9 extra damage.
>
>
> ***Escape Artist*** The ratfolk can cast Darkness, Spider Climb or Smoke Shroud, Silence while frightened by its Scurry Away feature
>
> ### Actions
>
> ***Multiattack.*** The ratfolk makes two attacks, it can replace any of it's attack with poisonous gift.
>
> ***Warp Star.*** range Spell Attack: +6 to hit, range 60 feet, one target. Hit: *Hit:* 9 (3d4 + 2) poison damage.  If the attack had advantage and the target is a creature, it must succeed on a DC 14 Constitution saving throw or be poisoned until the end of the turn of it's next turn.
>
> ***Shortsword.*** Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 6 (1d6 + 3) Slashing.
>
> ***Spellcasting.*** The keeper casts one of the following spells, using Wisdom as its spellcasting ability (spell save DC 14, +6 to hit with spell attacks):
>
> At will: Prestidigitation, Mage Hand, Minor Illusion.
>
> 2/day each: Darkness, Spider Climb, Smoke Shroud, Silence
>
> 1/day each: Haste, Pass Without Trace
>
>
> ### Reactions
>
> ***Scurry Away.***  When an enemy the ratfolk can see ends its turn, the ratfolk can move up to half its speed away from the creature and become frightened of it until the end of the creature's next turn. This movement doesn't provoke opportunity attacks. While frightened this way, the ratfolk can only take the dash, disengage, dodge, or hide action. This feature ignores immunity to the frightened condition.

> ## Master Assassin
> Medium humanoid, any non-good alignment
> ___
> - **Armor Class** 19
> - **Hit Points** 91 (14d8+28)
> - **Speed** 50 ft Climb 50 ft.
>___
>|STR|DEX|CON|INT|WIS|CHA|
>|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|
>|11 (+0)|20 (+5)|14 (+2)|14 (+2)|18 (+4)|12 (+1)|
>___
> - **Saving Throws** Dex +9, Int +6, Wis +8, Cha +5
> - **Skills** Acrobatics +9, Investigation +6, Perception +11, Sleight of hand +9, Stealth +13,
> - **Damage Resistance** Poison
> - **Senses** darkvision 60 ft., blindsight 10 ft., passive Perception 21
> - **Languages** Common, Thieves' cant, Undercommon
> - **Challenge** 11 (3,900 XP) &emsp;&emsp;&emsp;&ensp;    **Proficiency Bonus** +4
> ___
>
> ***Unarmored Defense*** While the ratfolk is wearing no armor and wielding no shield, its AC includes its Wisdom modifier.
>
> ***Nimble Reflex.*** When a creature 30 or more feet away form the ratfolk makes ranged attack against it and it can see the attack and it is not grappled incapacitated or restrained, the attack is made with disadvantage.
>
> ***Evasion.*** If the ratfolk is subjected to an effect that allows it to make a Dexterity saving throw to take only half damage, the assassin instead takes no damage if it succeeds on the saving throw, and only half damage if it fails.
>
> ***Assassinate.*** During its first turn, the ratfolk has advantage on attack rolls against any creature that hasn't taken a turn. Any hit the ratfolk scores against a surprised creature is a critical hit.
>
> ***Crevice Crawler.*** The ratfolk can move through and stop in a space large enough to fit a creature one size smaller than it without squeezing.
>
> ***Keen Smell.*** The ratfolk has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on Smell.
>
> ***Overwhelming Mass (4).*** The first time each turn that the ratfolk hits a creature with an attack, it deals an extra 1 damage per ally it has within 5 feet of its target that isn't incapacitated. The ratfolk can benefit from up to 3 allies this way.
>
> ***Sneak Attack (1/Turn).*** The ratfolk deals an extra 21 (6d6) damage when it hits a target with a weapon attack and has advantage on the attack roll, or when the target is within 5 ft. of an ally of the assassin that isn't incapacitated and the ratfolk doesn't have disadvantage on the attack roll.
>
> ***Poisoned Blades*** At the end of each turn, each creature the hit by the ratfolk with a Throwing Star or Shortsword attack must make a DC 16 Constitution saving throw or take 14 (4d6) poison damage on a failed save for each time it was hit by the ratfolk this turn, or half as much damage on a successful one.
>
> ### Bonus Action
> ***Cunning Action.*** The ratfolk takes the Dash, Disengage, or Hide 
action
>
> ### Actions
>
> ***Multiattack.*** The ratfolk makes three attacks.
>
> ***Shortsword.*** Melee Weapon Attack: +9 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 8 (1d6 + 5) Slashing.
>
> ***Throwing Star.*** Ranged Weapon Attack: +9 to hit, range 20/60 ft., one target. Hit: 8 (1d6 + 5). If the target is a creature and the attack did not have disadvantage, the next melee weapon attack the ratfolk makes against the target this turn has advantage. 
>
> ***Unarmed Strike.*** Melee Weapon Attack: +9 to hit, reach 5 ft., one 
target. Hit: 8 (1d6 + 5) pricing damage.
>
> ### Reactions
>
> ***Scurry Away.*** When an enemy the ratfolk can see ends its turn, the ratfolk can move up to half its speed away from the creature and become frightened of it until the end of the creature's next turn. This movement doesn't provoke opportunity attacks. While frightened this way, the ratfolk can only take the Dash, Disengage, Dodge, or Hide action. This feature ignores immunity to the frightened condition.
