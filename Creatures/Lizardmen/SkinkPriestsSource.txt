> ## Skink Conduit
> *Medium humanoid (skink), lawful neutral*
> ___
> - **Armor Class** 15 (natural armor)
> - **Hit Points** 55 (10d8 + 10)
> - **Speed** 35 ft., swim 30 ft., climb 25 ft.
>___
>|STR|DEX|CON|INT|WIS|CHA|
>|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|
>|10 (0)|14 (+2)|12 (+1)|14 (+2)|14 (+2)|12 (+1)|
>___
> - **Saving Throws** Dex +4, Con +3, Int +4, Wis +4
> - **Skills** Arcana +4, Nature +4, Perception +4, Stealth +4, Survival +4, Religion +4
> - **Senses** darkvision 30 ft., passive Perception 14
> - **Languages** Draconic
> - **Challenge** 3 (700 XP) &emsp;&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;&emsp; **Proficiency Bonus** +2
> ___
>
> ***Arcane Vessel.*** When a celestial, fiend, or dragon casts a spell on its turn within 100 feet of the skink and can see it, it can cast the spell as if it were in the skink's space instead of its own, using the skink conduit's senses. The skink must be willing to allow this, and if the spell cast this way requires concentration, the skink can choose to maintain concentration on the spell in the other creature's place if the skink isn't already concentrating on a spell.
>
> ***Cold Blooded.*** The skink has advantage on saving throws against being charmed or frightened.
>
> ***Eldritch Mind.*** The skink has advantage on Constitution saving throws it makes to maintain concentration spells.
>
> ***Evasion.*** When the skink makes a Dexterity saving throw to reduce the damage of an effect by half, it instead takes no damage on a success and only half damage on a failure.
>
> ***Hold Breath.*** The skink can hold its breath for 1 hour.
>
> ***Lore of Magic.*** The skink is specialized in one of the following lores of magic: Heavens or Beasts. The chosen lore determines one of its traits the spells it knows.
>
> ***Primeval Mask.*** The skink conduit has advantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks made to hide using foliage or a creature larger than it and may do so even if it is only lightly obscured while hiding this way.
>
> ### Actions
>
> ***Quarterstaff.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +2 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 3 (1d6) bludgeoning damage, or 4 (1d8) bludgeoning damage if wielded with two hands.
>
> ***Spellcasting.*** The skink is a spellcaster. Its spellcasting ability is Wisdom (spell save DC 13, +4 to hit with spell attacks). The spells the skink knows depend on the lore of magic it has specialized in, as described in its Lore of Magic trait.

### Skink Conduit <Br> Lore of Magic Options 
Select one of the following lores or magic to determine a skink conduit's additional passive trait and spell list. <Br>
**New spells listed at the end of the document*

<Br>

> #### Skink Conduit: Lore of Beasts
>
> ##### **Trait:**
> ***Wild Heart.*** Whenever the skink targets only itself or one monstrosity, giant, dragon, or fey with an Intelligence of 8 or less with a spell that requires concentration, it can choose for the spell to no longer require concentration. The spell ends early if the skink is incapacitated or uses this feature to alter another spell.  <br><br>
>
> ##### **Spell List:**
> **At will:** *guidance*, *primal savagery*, *thaumaturgy* <br>
> **1/Day:** *amber spear**, *pass without trace*,  *wild surge**

> #### Skink Conduit: Lore of Heavens
>
> ##### **Trait:**
> ***Roiling Skies.*** Creatures flying 5 or more feet above a surface have disadvantage saving throws against spells cast by the skink, and the skink has advantage on spell attack rolls against them.  <br><br>
>
> ##### **Spell List:**
> **At will:** *guidance*, *shocking grasp*, *thaumaturgy* <br>
> **1Day:** *curse of the midnight wind**, *lighting strike**, *wind blast**
