> ## Exalted Bloodthirster
>*Huge fiend (demon), chaotic evil*
> ___
> - **Armor Class** 18 (Plate)
> - **Hit Points** 310 (27d12+135)
> - **Speed** 40ft., fly 50 ft.
>___
>|STR|DEX|CON|INT|WIS|CHA|
>|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|
>|24 (+7)|20 (+5)|20 (+5)|18 (+4)|20 (+5)|20 (+5)|
>___
> - **Saving Throws** Con +10, Dex +10, Int +5, Wis +10,
> - **Skills** Acrobatics +10, Athletics +19, intimidation +11, Perception +11
> - **Damage Resistances** cold, lightning; bludgeoning, 
piercing, and slashing from nonmagical attacks
> - **Damage Immunities** poison, fire
> - **Condition Immunities** charmed, frightened, poisoned
> - **Senses** Blindsight 10 Ft., Truesight 120 ft., Darkvision 300 ft., passive Perception 21
> - **Languages** Abyssal, telepathy 300 ft.
> - **Challenge** 20 (18,000 XP)   &emsp;&emsp; **Proficiency Bonus** +6        
> ___
>
> ***The Land Rebels.*** Bloodthirster ignores difficult terrain, in addition any ground the Bloodthirster moves though becomes difficult terrain, and is set ablaze with fire for the next minute, A non-demon creature takes the 10(3d6) fire when damage it enters the area for the first time on a turn or ends its turn there.
>
> ***Magic Resistance.*** The Bloodthirster has advantage on saving throws 
against spells and other magical effects.
>
> **Legendary Resistance** (3/Day) If the Bloodthirster fails a saving throw, it
can choose to succeed instead.
>
> ***Weapons of Fury.*** Any weapons the Bloodthirster wielding becomes magical for long as it wields it and Bloodthirster can't be disarmed of the weapon.
>
> ***SpellBreaker*** At the start of each of Bloodthirster turns, it can end one spell of 6th level or lower of it's choice effecting it or a creature with in 10 feet of it that it can see. It can do this even while incapacitated.
>___
> ### Bonus Actions
> ***Cleave.***  When the Bloodthirster reduces a creature to 0 hit points with it's Greataxe of Fury on its turn, the Bloodthirster can take a bonus action to Greataxe of Fury.
>
> ***Aggressive.*** The Bloodthirster moves up to its speed toward a hostile creature 
it can see.
>___
> ### Actions
> ***Multiattack.*** The keeper makes three attacks.
>
> ***Lash of Khorne.*** Melee Weapon Attack: +13 to hit, reach 60 ft. , one  target. Hit: 20 (3d8 + 7) slashing damage. The target must make a DC 21 strength saving throw or be pulled 30 feet towards the Bloodthirster.
>
> **Greataxe of Khorne.** Melee Weapon Attack: +13 to hit, reach 10ft., one target. Hit: 27 (3d12 + 7) slashing damage. When a creature that is concentrating on a spell is damage by the Greataxe, that creature has disadvantage on the saving throw it makes to maintain its concentration.
>
> **Whirlwind of Rage** the Bloodthirster makes a **Greataxe of Fury.** attack against any number of creatures within 10 feet of it, with a separate attack roll for each target. The area with in 10 feet of the Bloodthirster is also effected by the ***The Land Rebels.*** feature above.
>
> ### Legendary Actions
> The stallion can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action option can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn. The Bloodthirster regains spent legendary actions at the start of its turn.
> 
> **Check.** The Bloodthirster makes a skill check that would normally require an action.
> 
> **Move.** The Bloodthirster moves up to half its speed without provoking attacks of opportunity.
>
> **Whip.** The Bloodthirster makes a Lash of Khorne attack.
> 
> **Greataxe. (Costs 2 Actions)** The Bloodthirster makes a Greataxe of Khorne attack.
>
> **Reconstitute (Costs w Actions).** If the Bloodthirster is suffering from one or more conditions that require it to make a saving throw to end, it may immediately make a save against each condition. A successful save for any of the throws will end the condition immediately. The only saving throws that this action can be used for are saves that are made at the end/start of its next turn or using its action. It can use this action while incapacitated.
>
> **Rejoice in the Slaughter (Costs 3 Actions)**  Each creature of the Bloodthirster's choice that is within 120 feet of it, that can hear or see it, gain advantage on their next attack roll until the end of the Bloodthirster's next turn. If the attack hits it is a critical hit if the attacker is within 10 feet of the creature.
>___
> ### Reactions
> ***Wrathful Charge.*** When the Bloodthirster is hit by a attack or a creature cast a spell that forces the Bloodthirster to make a saving throw to resist its effects, the Bloodthirster can move up to half it speed towards the creature without provoking opportunity attacks, the Bloodthirster can then make a melee weapon attack against that creature with advantage if it is with in 10 feet of it.

> ## Skulltaker
>*Medium fiend (demon), chaotic evil*
> ___
> - **Armor Class** 18 (natural armor)
> - **Hit Points** 190 (20d8+100)
> - **Speed** 50ft.
>___
>|STR|DEX|CON|INT|WIS|CHA|
>|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|
>|20 (+5)|20 (+5)|20 (+5)|12 (+1)|16 (+3)|14 (+2)|
>___
> - **Saving Throws** Str +10 Con +10, Dex +10, Wis +8
> - **Skills** Athletics +11, Intimidation +12, Perception +8
> - **Damage Resistances** cold, fire, lightning
> - **Damage Immunities** poison
> - **Condition Immunities** charmed, frightened, poisoned 
> - **Senses** Blindsight 10 Ft., darkvision 120 ft., passive Perception 18
> - **Languages** Abyssal, telepathy 60 ft.
> - **Challenge** 13 (700 XP)   &emsp;&emsp;&emsp;&ensp;  **Proficiency Bonus** +5      
> ___
>
> **Spell Bane (3/Day)** If The Skulltaker fails a saving throw against spells and other magical effects, it
can choose to succeed instead.
>
> ***Brute.*** A melee weapon deals one extra die of his damage when the Skulltaker hits with it (included in the attack).
>
> ***Magic Resistance.*** The Skulltaker has advantage on saving throws 
against spells and other magical effects.
>
>
> ### Bonus Action
>***Irresistible Challenge.*** Skulltaker marks creature that he can see or hear with in 120 feet of him. The marked bestow the fallowing effects.
>
> * The marked creature has disadvantage on attack rolls against creatures other than Skulltaker and the Skulltaker has disadvantage on attack against creatures that are not marked.
> * The marked target must make a DC 18 Charisma each time it attempts to move away from Skulltaker, if it succeeds on this saving throw, mark doesn’t restrict the target’s movement for that turn.  
> * If the creature tries to use teleportation or interplanar travel, it must first make a DC 18 Charisma saving throw. On a success, the creature can use that magic. On a failure, the creature wastes the use of the spell or effect. 
> * The creatures flying speed (if any) is reduced to 0 feet while it is marked. An airborne creature affected by the mark descends at 60 feet per round until it reaches the ground or the mark ends. The mark ends when the marked creature or the Skulltaker die or the Skulltaker marks another creature.
>
> ### Actions
> ***Multiattack.*** The Skulltaker makes two attacks. 
>
> ***Slayer Sword.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +10 to hit, reach 5ft., one target. *Hit:* 19 (2d12 + 5) slashing damage. If the target is marked by its Skulltaker and it rolled a 20 on the attack roll, the target must make a DC 18 Dexterity saving throw or take extra 26(4d12) slashing damage 
from the hit. If it fails by 10 more it and target is smaller then huge and has at least one head, the target is decapitated and dies if it can't survive without that head.
>
>
>### Reactions
>
> ***Parry.*** The Skulltaker adds 5 to his AC against one melee attack that would hit it. To do so, the Skulltaker must see the attacker and be wielding a melee weapon.
>
> ### Legendary Actions
> The Skulltaker can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action option can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn. The Skulltaker regains spent legendary actions at the start of its turn.
> 
> **Check.** The Skulltaker makes a skill check that would normally require an action.
> 
> **Martial Prowess.** The Skulltaker may move up to half its speed towards marked creature without provoking attacks of opportunity, he may make a Slayer Sword attack against it if the creature is with in reach.
>
> **Reconstitute.** If the Skulltaker is suffering from a conditions that requires him to make a saving throw to end it, he may immediately make a save against the condition. A successful save ends the condition immediately. The only saving throws that this action can be used for are saves that are made at the end/start of its next turn or using its action. He can use this action while incapacitated.
>
> **Crippling Strike (Costs 2 Actions).** The Skulltaker make a Slayer Sword attack. If the attack his it must succeed on a DC 18 strength saving throw or be knocked prone and it's speed becomes 0 until the end of it's next turn.
> 
> **Furious Strike. (Costs 2 Actions)** A creature with in 5 feet if him must make DC 18 dexterity throw, on failure it can’t take reactions until the start of its next turn and the Skulltaker makes a Slayer Sword attack with advantage, on a success the creature can take reactions and the Slayer Sword attack is made with out advantage.
>

> ## Karanak
>*large fiend (demon), chaotic evil*
> ___
> - **Armor Class** 17 (natural armor)
> - **Hit Points** 210 (20d10+100)
> - **Speed** 60ft.
>___
>|STR|DEX|CON|INT|WIS|CHA|
>|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|
>|20 (+5)|18 (+4)|20 (+5)|12 (+1)|20 (+5)|12 (+1)|
>___
> - **Saving Throws** In +6, Cha +6 Wis +10
> - **Skills** Perception +15
> - **Damage Resistances** cold, fire, lightning
> - **Damage Immunities** poison
> - **Condition Immunities** blinded, charmed, deafened, exhaustion, 
frightened, poisoned, stunned.
> - **Senses**  Truesight 30 ft,, darkvision 120 ft., passive Perception 25
> - **Languages** Understands Abyssal but can't speak it.
> - **Challenge** 15 (1,800 XP)   &emsp;&emsp;&ensp;  **Proficiency Bonus** +5        
> ___
>
> ***Faultless Tracker.*** Karanak is given a quarry by its master. The quarry can be a specific creature or general type of creature the master is has seen before. Karanak knows the direction and distance to its quarry as long as the two of them are on the same plane of existence. If the quarry is on another plan of existence, Karanak knows what plan it is on. Karanak can have only one such quarry at a time. The Karanak also always knows the location of its master.
>
> ***Brass Collar of Bloody Vengeance.*** The Karanak has advantage on saving throws against spells, spell attacks against it are made with disadvantage, and it has resistance to damage caused by spells. If Karanak succeeds on a saving throw or spell attack miss it, the caster of the it takes 1d6 psychic damage per level of the spell. 
>
> ***Multiheaded.*** Karanak can't be surprised, and it has advantage on saving throws against being knocked unconscious. 
>
> ***Keen Hearing and Smell.*** The Karanak has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on hearing or smell.
>
> ***Pack Tactics.*** The Karanak has advantage on attack rolls against a creature if at least one of the hound's allies is within 5 feet of the creature and the ally isn't incapacitated.
>
> ### Actions
> ***Multiattack.*** The Karanak makes three attacks 
>
> ***Bite.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +10 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 14 (2d8 + 5) piercing damage or 18 (3d8 + 5) 
if it is Karanak's quarry. If the target is a creature, it must succeed on a DC 18 Strength saving throw, first time a creature fails this saving throw in a turn, it is knocked prone, the second time in a turn, the creature is incapacitated until the end of it's next turn, third time in a turn, the creature 55(10d10) force damage and is paralyzed until the end of it's next turn. If the force damage reduces the target to 0 hit points the target is ripped limb form limb and dies.
>
> ***Claws.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +10 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 10 (2d4 + 5) slashing damage.
>
> ***Burning Roar (Recharge 5–6).*** The Karanak lets out a roar that burns the air in a 60-foot cone in front of it.  Each creature in that area must make a DC 18 Constitution saving throw, taking 21 (6d6) fire damage plus 21 (6d6) thunder damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
>
> ### Legendary Actions
> The Karanak can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action option can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn. The Karanak regains spent legendary actions at the start of its turn.
> 
> **Check.** Karanak makes a skill check that would normally require an action.
>
> **Claw.** Karanak makes a claw attack.
>
> **Reconstitute.** If Karanak is suffering from a conditions that requires it to make a saving throw to end it, it may immediately make a save against the condition. A successful save ends the condition immediately. The only saving throws that this action can be used for are saves that are made at the end/start of its next turn or using its action. It can use this action while incapacitated.
> 
> **Chase Down (Costs 2 Actions).** Karanak moves up to its speed it's toward it's quarry, without provoking attacks of opportunity.
>
> **Call of the Hunt (Costs 3 Actions).** If Karanak lets our howl that can be heard up to ten miles away, if Karanak's see it's quarry and it can hear the howl, it must make a DC 18 charisma saving throw or A Flesh Hound appears in unoccupied spaces within 30 feet of the it and remains until destroyed or Karanak dies or leaves this plan of existenc. a Flesh Hound summoned in this way rolls initiative and act in the next available turn, they also lose their Burning Roar feature and may only attack Karanak's quarry. The Karanak can have up to three Flesh Hounds summoned by this ability at a time. 
>

## Demons of Rage in Your Adventures
The demons in this document are based on the daemons of Khorne from the Warhammer franchise.  However, they can be adapted to fit any setting which features bloodthirster, warmongering demons.  In more traditional Dungeons and Dragons settings, they could serve as demonic warriors spawned to fight in the Blood War or as minions in service to one of the more warmongering demon lords such as Baphomet.  The following tables provide you with examples of ways to incorporate these demons into your adventures.
