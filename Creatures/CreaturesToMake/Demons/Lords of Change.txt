> ## Exalted Lord of Change
>*Huge fiend (demon), chaotic evil*
> ___
> - **Armor Class** 16 (natural armor)
> - **Hit Points** 283 (27d12+108)
> - **Speed** 40ft. fly 80 ft.
>___
>|STR|DEX|CON|INT|WIS|CHA|
>|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|
>|18 (+4)|18 (+4)|18 (+4)|25 (+7)|22 (+6)|22 (+6)|
>___
> - **Saving Throws** Con +10, CHA +12, Wis +12, Int +13
> - **Skills** Arcana +19, History +19 Deception +10, Insight +10, Perception +12
> - **Damage Resistances** cold, lightning; bludgeoning, 
piercing, and slashing from nonmagical attacks
> - **Damage Immunities** poison, fire
> - **Condition Immunities** poisoned
> - **Senses** truesight 120 ft., darkvision 300 ft., passive Perception 22
> - **Languages**  all, telepathy 300 ft.
> - **Challenge** 17 (18,000 XP)   &emsp;&emsp; **Proficiency Bonus** +6        
> ___
>
> ***Distant Caster.*** When Lord of Change cast a spell that has a range of 5 feet or greater, double the range of the spell.
 When Lord of Change cast a spell that has a range of touch, The range of the spell 30 feet.
>
> ***Improved Spell Casting.*** When Lord of Change cast an spell that requires it to make an ability check as a part of casting that spell (as in Counterspell, Dispel Magic, Chaos Strom), it ads it's proficiency bonus to that ability check.
>
> **Resistance Shredder** Lord of Change's spells it casts ignore resistance. 
>
> ***Warp Flame.*** When a creature is reduced to 0 hit points by of the Lord of Change's spells, that creature must make a DC 21 Charisma saving throw at the a end of it's next turn if it is still has 0 hit points, if the target fails it dies and instantly transforms into a living Pink Horror of Tzeentch under the Lord of Change's control. The transformation of the body can be undone only by a wish spell.
>
> ***Magic Resistance.*** The Lord of Change has advantage on saving throws 
against spells and other magical effects.
>
> **Legendary Resistance** (3/Day) If the Lord of Change fails a saving throw, it
can choose to succeed instead.
>
> ***Dual Minded*** If the Lord of Change attempts to cast a spell that requires concentration while already concentrating on an existing spell, it can maintain concentration on both spells simultaneously. if it needs to make a Concentration saving throw to maintain concentration on both spells, it makes the save with advantage. On a failure it loses concentration on one of the spells. (it's choice)
>
> ### Actions
> ***Multiattack.*** The keeper makes three attacks: one with its 
Baleful Sword, one with it's staff, one with it's beak, Alternatively, it can replace any of it's attacks to cast a at will spell.
>
>
> ***Staff of Tzeentch.*** Melee Weapon Attack: +10 to hit, reach 10 ft. , one target. Hit: 19 (4d6 + 4) bludgeoning damage and if the target is a creature, it has disadvantage on it's next saving throw until the end of the Lord of Change's next turn.
>
> ***Beak.*** Melee Weapon Attack: +10 to hit, reach 10 ft. , one target. Hit: 19 (4d6 + 5) piercing damage.
>
> ***Baleful Sword.*** Melee Weapon Attack: +10 to hit, reach 10 ft. , one  target. Hit: 17 (3d8 + 4) slashing damage plus 13 (3d8) fire damage.
>
> ***Spellcasting.*** The keeper casts one of the following spells, requiring no components and using intelligence as its spellcasting ability (spell save DC 21):
>
>***At will:*** *Burning hands (5th level)*, *Dissonant Whispers (6th level)* *Aganazzar's Scorcher (4th level)* *Flaming Sphere (4th level)* **Glean Magic (7th level) *, *Dispel magic* *Nystul's Magic Aura(self only)*, *Invisibility*, *Banishment* *Bolt of Change*
>
> 3/day each: *Counter Spell* *Shield* *Dimension Door* *Arcane Eye*
>
>***1/day each:***  *Fire Storm* *Mind Blank* *Prismatic Spray* *Chaos Strom*,  
>
> ### Bonus Actions
> ***Dimensional Shift.***   The Lord of Change teleports up to 30 feet to an unoccupied space that it can see.
> 
> ### Legendary Actions
> The Lord of Change can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action option can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn. The Lord of Change regains spent legendary actions at the start of its turn.
> 
> **Check.** The Lord of Change makes a skill check that would normally require an action.
> 
> **Move.** The Lord of Change moves up to half its speed without provoking attacks of opportunity.
>
> **Staff.** The Lord of Change makes a Staff of Tzeentch attack.
> 
> **Sword. (Costs 2 Actions)** The Lord of Change makes a Baleful Sword attack.
>
> **Reconstitute (Costs 2 Actions).** If the Lord of Change is suffering from one or more conditions that require it to make a saving throw to end, it may immediately make a save against each condition. A successful save for any of the throws will end the condition immediately. The only saving throws that this action can be used for are saves that are made at the end/start of its next turn or using its action. It can use this action while incapacitated.
>
> **Beacon of Sorcery (Costs 3 Actions)**  Each creature of the Exalted Lord of Change's choice that is within 120 feet of it, that can hear or see it, they may cast a spell 4th level or lower on their next turn that has a casting time of 1 action as a bonus action.
>

> ___
>
> ***Shifting Defences.*** Whenever The Changeling makes a saving throw, if their is a creature with in 10 feet of it that proficiency in that saving throw, it gets +5 saving throw.
>
> ***Magic Resistance.*** The Changeling has advantage on saving throws 
against spells and other magical effects.
>
> **Legendary Resistance** (3/Day) If The Changeling fails a saving throw, it
can choose to succeed instead.
>
> **Shifting Veil  .** The Changeling is immune to magic that allows other creatures to read its thoughts, determine whether it is lying, know it alignment, or know its creature type.
>
> ### Actions
> ***Multiattack.*** The Changeling makes three attacks. 
>
> ***The Trickster’s Staff.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +10 to hit, reach 5ft., one target. *Hit:* 12 (2d6 + 5) bludgeoning damage.
>
> ***Formless’s strike.*** *The changeling chooses a creature with in 120 feet of it that it can see, The changeling then takes the attack action as though it was an exact copy of the creature expect for it still has it's current Hit Points, senses, size, and resistance. It is copy of the creature until it finishes the action> It makes any attacks this way as though it were made with any Simple weapon, Martial weapon, improvised weapon, or unarmed strike it has seen the creature wield in the last hour. It can provide any amuintion required for the attacks. all damage dealt this way becomes force damage.
>
> ***Change Shape.*** The Changeling polymorphs into any Medium or smaller creature that it has seen. its statistics, other than his size, are the same in each form. Any equipment it is wearing or carrying isn't transformed.
>
> ***Spellcasting.*** The Changeling casts one of the following spells, requiring no components and using Intelligence as its spellcasting ability (spell save DC 20):
>
> ***At will:*** detect thoughts, Mage Hand, Glean Magic(5th level).
>
> ***1/day each:*** *Pass Without Trace*, *Nondetection*(self only),
>
> ### Legendary Actions
> The Changeling can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action option can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn. The Changeling regains spent legendary actions at the start of her turn.
>
> **Check.** The Changeling makes a skill check that would normally require an action. 
>
> **Mimic Movement.** The Changeling chooses a creature with in rage, it moves up the chosen creatures movement speed with without provoking attacks of opportunity. The Changeling can't take this legendary actions again until she regains spent legendary actions
>
> **Formless’s strikes.** (Costs 2 Actions) Changeling uses it Formless’s strike action.
>
> **Reconstitute (Costs 2 Actions).** If The Changeling is suffering from one or more conditions that require her to make a saving throw to end, it may immediately make a save against each condition. A successful save for any of the throws will end the condition immediately. The only saving throws that this action can be used for are saves that are made at the end/start of its next turn or using its action. It can use this action while incapacitated.
>
> **Mimic Magic (Costs 2 Actions).** The Changeling casts Glean Magic or spell that is has stolen with Glean Magic that has cast time of one action requiring no components for the spell.
>
>___
> ### Reactions
> ***Adaptive Barrier.*** - When The Changeling takes damage, it gains resistance to the triggering damage type until the start of it's next turn.
