> ## The Masque
>*Medium fiend (demon), chaotic evil*
> ___
> - **Armor Class** 18 (natural armor)
> - **Hit Points** 110 (10d8+60)
> - **Speed** 80ft.
>___
>|STR|DEX|CON|INT|WIS|CHA|
>|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|
>|14 (+2)|22 (+6)|22 (+6)|12 (+1)|14 (+2)|25 (+7)|
>___
> - **Saving Throws** Dex +10, Con +10
> - **Skills** Acrobatics +16, Performance +17 
> - **Damage Resistances** cold, fire, lightning
> - **Damage Immunities** poison 
> - **Condition Immunities** charmed, exhaustion, frightened, grappled, paralyzed, petrified, poisoned, prone, restrained, stunned.
> - **Senses** darkvision 120 ft., passive Perception 12
> - **Languages** Abyssal, telepathy 60 ft.
> - **Challenge** 13 (700 XP)   &emsp;&emsp;&emsp;&ensp;  **Proficiency Bonus** +5       
> ___
> ***Freedom of Movement.*** The Masque ignores difficult terrain, 
and magical effects can't reduce her speed.
>
> ***Reliable Talent*** Whenever The Masque makes an ability check that lets her add her proficiency bonus, she can treat a d20 roll of 9 or lower as a 10.
>
> ***Lithe and Swift*** The Masque gains a +5 bonus to initiative.
>
> ***Magic Resistance.*** The Masque has advantage on saving throws 
against spells and other magical effects.
>
> **Legendary Resistance** (3/Day) If The Masque fails a saving throw, she
can choose to succeed instead.
>
> ***Avoidance.*** If the Masque is subjected to an effect that allows 
her to make a saving throw to take only half damage, she instead 
takes no damage if she succeeds on the saving throw, and only 
half damage if she fails. 
>
> **The Eternal Dance.** At the end of The Masque turn, each creature with in 90 feet of the The Masque must succeed on a DC 20 Wisdom saving throw or become part of the Eternal Dance until the end of the Masque next turn. While a creature is part of Dance, it can't willingly move away from the Masque.
> 
>
> ### Actions
> ***Multiattack.*** The Masque makes two attacks. 
>
> ***Claw.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +11 to hit, reach 5ft., one target. *Hit:* 15 (2d8 + 6) slashing damage.
>
> ### Legendary Actions
> The Masque can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action option can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn. The Masque regains spent legendary actions at the start of her turn.
>
> **Check.** The Masque makes a skill check that would normally require an action. 
>
> **Dancing of the Dreaming** The Masque and each creature in the Eternal Dance it is pushed up to 30 along the ground in a direction she chooses (she can choose a different directions for each creature). If the creature is flying, it is knocked Prone instead. The Masque can't take this legendary actions again until she regains spent legendary actions
> 
> **Waltz of Lethargy** The Masque and each creature in the Eternal Dance must make a DC 20 constitution saving throw, taking 14 (4d6) force damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. If a creature fails the save by 10 or more, it also gains a level of Exhaustion. The Masque can't take this legendary actions again until she regains spent legendary actions
> 
> **Fleshspasm Polka.** The Masque and each creature in Eternal Dance must make a melee attack against a creature other than itself that The Masque choose. The Masque can't take this legendary actions again until she regains spent legendary actions
>
> **Reconstitute (Costs 2 Actions).** If The Masque is suffering from one or more conditions that require her to make a saving throw to end, it may immediately make a save against each condition. A successful save for any of the throws will end the condition immediately. The only saving throws that this action can be used for are saves that are made at the end/start of its next turn or using its action. It can use this action while incapacitated.
>___
> ### Reactions
> ***Redirect.*** When a creature the Masque can see targets her with an attack or a spell or effect that targets only her, the she chooses another creature apart of the Eternal Dance within 5 feet of it. The two swap places, and the chosen creature becomes the target instead.

On a successful saving throw, the creature is immune to the charming aura of Synessas for 1 hour.

>

>
>***Multiattack.*** Synessa make two attacks with Core of Sorrows, she can replace any of these attacks to cast a spell that require action, or Teleport.
>
>
>***Core of Sorrows.*** ranged Weapon Attack: +13 to hit, range 120 ft., one target. Hit: 46  (6d12 + 7) acid damage.
>
>***Teleport.*** Synessa magically teleports, along with any equipment 
she is wearing or carrying, up to 120 feet to an unoccupied 
space he can see.
>
>

>

>
>***3/day each:***   counterspell, project Image, dominate monster, Compulsion, Hypnotic Pattern, Scrying.
>___
> ### Reactions
> ***Instinctive Gile.*** when a Creature  Synessa can see within 120 feet of makes an attack roll against her, The attacker must make a DC 21 Charisma saving throw. On a failed save, the attacker must target another creature with in range of the attack other then Synessa, If multiple creatures are with in range, the attacker chooses which one to target, if no other creature are with in range of the attack, the attack is wasted.
