> ## Chaos Dwarf Warrior
> *Medium humanoid, lawful evil*
> ___
> - **Armor Class** 16 (ring mail, shield)
> - **Hit Points** 39 (6d8 + 12)
> - **Speed** 25 ft.
> ___
>|STR|DEX|CON|INT|WIS|CHA|
>|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|
>|14 (+2)|9 (-1)|14 (+2)|12 (+1)|10 (+0)|12 (+1)|
>___
> - **Damage Resistances** poison
> - **Skills** Athletics +4, Intimidation +3
> - **Senses** darkvision 30 ft., passive Perception 10
> - **Languages** Dwarvish
> - **Challenge** 1 (200 XP)
> - **Proficiency Bonus** +2
> ___
>
> ***Debilitating Strikes.*** When the dwarf reduces a creature to 0 hit points with an attack, that creature is stunned for the next minute, even if it regains hit points.
>
> ***Dwarven Resilience.*** The dwarf has advantage on saving throws against poison.
>
> ***Shield Fighter.*** While the dwarf is wielding a shield, its melee weapon attacks deal an extra 3 (1d6) damage when the dwarf hits with them (included in the attack).
> 
> ### Actions
>
> ***Battleaxe.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 10 (1d8 + 1d6 + 2) slashing damage.
> 
> ### Bonus Actions
>
> ***Resolute Stance.*** At the start of the dwarf's turn, it can focus entirely on standing its ground mentally and physically. Until the start of its next turn, any attack roll made against the dwarf has disadvantage. Additionally, the dwarf makes Strength (Athletics) checks and all saving throws with advantage, except Dexterity saving throws, which it makes with disadvantage. The dwarf loses this benefit if an effect moves it against its will or knocks it prone. If the dwarf uses this feature, its speed becomes 0 until the end of the current turn.

> ## Dwarf Blazing Beard Warrior
> *Medium humanoid, lawful evil*
> ___
> - **Armor Class** 16 (ring mail, shield)
> - **Hit Points** 65 (10d8 + 20)
> - **Speed** 25 ft.
>___
>|STR|DEX|CON|INT|WIS|CHA|
>|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|
>|14 (+2)|9 (-1)|14 (+2)|12 (+1)|10 (+0)|12 (+1)|
>___
> - **Saving Throws** Wis +2
> - **Damage Resistances** fire, poison
> - **Skills** Athletics +4, Intimidation +3
> - **Senses** darkvision 30 ft., passive Perception 10
> - **Languages** Dwarvish
> - **Challenge** 2 (450 XP)
> - **Proficiency Bonus** +2
> ___
>
> ***Burning Fury.*** When the dwarf hits a target with a weapon attack, the weapon deals an extra 3 (1d6) fire damage if the target is on fire, and another 3 (1d6) fire damage if the dwarf is on fire.
>
> ***Burning Resolve.*** When the dwarf uses its Resolute feature, each creature and flammable object within 10 feet of it must make a DC 12 Dexterity saving throw. On a failed save, the creature or object ignites in flames. Until a creature takes an action to douse the fire, the creature or object takes 3 (1d6) fire damage at the end of each of its turns.
>
> ***Debilitating Strikes.*** When the dwarf reduces a creature to 0 hit points with an attack, that creature is stunned for the next minute, even if it regains hit points.
>
> ***Dwarven Resilience.*** The dwarf has advantage on saving throws against poison.
>
> ***Shield Fighter.*** While the dwarf is wielding a shield, its melee weapon attacks deal an extra 3 (1d6) damage when the dwarf hits with them (included in the attack).
> 
> ### Actions
> ***Battleaxe.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 10 (1d8 + 1d6 + 2) slashing damage plus 3 (1d6) fire damage if the target is on fire.
> 
> ***Naphtha Bomb (1/day).*** If the dwarf is not restrained or grappled, it hurls a bomb at a point it can see within 30 feet that is not behind total cover. The bomb detonates at this point, and each creature within 10 feet must make a DC 14 Dexterity saving throw. On a failure, a creature takes 10 (3d6) fire damage and ignites in flames (as described in Burning Resolve). On a success, a creature takes half as much damage and doesn't ignite. <br>
> When the dwarf attempts to throw the bomb, creatures can use their reaction to make attacks of opportunity against it as though it moved out of their reach. If a creature hits the dwarf this way, the dwarf drops the bomb, and it detonates at the dwarf's location.
>
> ### Bonus Actions
>
> ***Resolute Stance.*** At the start of the dwarf's turn, it can focus entirely on standing its ground mentally and physically. Until the start of its next turn, any attack roll made against the dwarf has disadvantage. Additionally, the dwarf makes Strength (Athletics) checks and all saving throws with advantage, except Dexterity saving throws, which it makes with disadvantage. The dwarf loses this benefit if an effect moves it against its will or knocks it prone. If the dwarf uses this feature, its speed becomes 0 until the end of the current turn.
