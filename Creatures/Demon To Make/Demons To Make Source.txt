> ***Multiattack.*** The keeper makes three attacks.

>
> **Whip.** The Bloodthirster makes a Lash of Khorne attack.
> 
> **Greataxe. (Costs 2 Actions)** The Bloodthirster makes a Greataxe of Khorne attack.
>
> **Rejoice in the Slaughter (Costs 3 Actions)**  Each creature of the Bloodthirster's choice that is within 120 feet of it, that can hear or see it, gain advantage on their next attack roll until the end of the Bloodthirster's next turn. If the attack hits it is a critical hit if the attacker is within 10 feet of the creature.
> 
> **Reconstitute (Costs 3 Actions).** If the Bloodthirster is suffering from one or more conditions that require it to make a saving throw to end, it may immediately make a save against each condition. A successful save for any of the throws will end the condition immediately. The only saving throws that this action can be used for are saves that are made at the end/start of its next turn or using its action. It can use this action while incapacitated.
>___
> ### Reactions
> ***Wrathful Charge.*** When the Bloodthirster is hit by a attack or a creature cast a spell that forces the Bloodthirster to make a saving throw to resist its effects, the Bloodthirster can move up to half it speed towards the creature without provoking opportunity attacks, the Bloodthirster can then make a melee weapon attack against that creature with advantage if it is with in 10 feet of it.
