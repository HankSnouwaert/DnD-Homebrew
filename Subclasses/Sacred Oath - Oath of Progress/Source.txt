 When a creature moves or is pushed or pulled into to a space within your aura you can use your reaction to force the creature to make a strength saving throw against your spell save DC which it can choose to fail. On a failure, the creature counties to move in the direction until it leaves your aura runs into a solid surface or creature, or you choose for it to stop.
 
You can also use your reaction to case a ranged weapon attack that pass in or out of the aura that you can see to deal an additional 1d6 damage on hit.

At 18th level, At the start of your turn you can choose to have your Tiny Servant or Summoned Construct you have created to emanate Aura of Acceleration until the start of you next turn rather then your self.
