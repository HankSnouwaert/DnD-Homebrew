# Roguish Archetype

## Shadow Dancer

### Living Shadow

When you choose this archetype at 3rd level, you gain the ability to cast the find familiar spell as action and with out components, once you cast find familiar this way, you can't do so until you finish a short or long rest. You must choose the creeping shadow as your familiar. It uses your modifiers and proficiency bonus for saving throws and checks and your AC for attacks against it, it also gains your Cunning Action feature. Later it gains Uncanny Dodge, Evasion, Reliable Talent, Blindsense, Elusive when you gain these features in the rouge class. When the shadow uses Uncanny Dodge, it takes no damage instead of halve and Evasion can be used when subjected to an effect that allows you to make any type of saving throw.

### Shadow Sight
When you choose this archetype at 3rd level, you gain darkvision out to a range of 60 feet. If you already have darkvision from your race, your creeping shadow and your darkvision range increases by 30 feet.

At 9th level you and your creeping shadow can see though magical darkness up to your darkvisions.

#### Shadow Strike
At 9th level, You can have your shadow copy your weapon strikes, As a bonus action, you can command your shadow to take the Attack action on its next turn as though it was making the attack with a weapon useing your attack modifiers and as though it was wielding weapon that you are holding and proficient with. It may also add your sneak attack damage if it hits, provide you have not used sneak attack on the turn you commanded it and that it meets the requirements for triggering sneak attack.

#### Shadowshift
Beginning at 13th level, As an action, you can cast the darkness spell centered on your self or your creeping shadow. You can do so a number of times equal to your proficiency bonus, and you regain all expended uses when you finish a long rest. Dexterity is your spellcasting ability for the spell.

In addition When you and your creeping shadow are in dim light or darkness and with in 60 feet of each other, you or your creeping shadow can magically swap places as a bonus action.

#### One with the Shadows
At 17th level, It is now near impossible to tell were your shadow starts and you end. You gain the Creeping Shadow's Amorphous, Shrouded in Darkness and spider Climb features. you may apply the Amorphous feature to any equipment you are wearing or carrying. You also have climb and swim speed equal to your walking speed you are also immune to grappled, restrained, and prone conditions.
