#### Lumimancer
Lumimancer
At 2nd level, You learn the Dancing Lights and Sacred Flame cantrip, which count as  wizard cantrips for you and doesn’t count against the number of wizard cantrips you know.  If you already know the Dancing Lights  cantrip, you may choose to learn another cantrip form the wizard spell list.
Additionally, the spell only requires verbel compents for casting  and no longer requires construction. lastly you may choose to have creatures of your choice you see in the light create by this spell be able to see though Magical darkness.
.

#### Umbral Stage
You can see normally in darkness, both magical and nonmagical, to a distance of 60 feet. If you already of darkvsion, you can see up to 120 feet

Starting at 2nd level, You learn the darkness spell, which counts as a wizard spell for you and does not count against the number of wizard spells you know. you add it to your spellbook, if it’s not there already.
You may choose to have darkness spell when you to cast it to not dispel magical light form your other wizard spells.


#### Shineshadow Snarl
Starting at 6th level, You learn the  Daylight which does not count against the number of wizard spells you know and counts as a wizard spell for you.  you add it to your spellbook, if it’s not there already. you may choose to have Daylight not dispel magical darkness that it overlaps with.
finally when a creature you can see takes damage with in 60 feet of you. you can use your reaction to reduce the damage by  1d6 +  intelligence modifier if the creature is in darkness created by one of your wizard spells or have the creature take radiant damage equal to the one by  1d6 +  intelligence modifier if it is in light created by one of your wizard spells.

#### Blot Out

Beginning at 10th level, You learn the  Shadow Of Moil spell which does not count against the number of wizard spells you know and counts as a wizard spell for you. you add it to your spellbook, if it’s not there already.  the spell only requires verbal components for casting it, when you cast the spell or at the start of your turn you may choose to have the spell not dim magical light created by your wizard spells.

Additionally you may cast the Darkness spell as bonus action and you can concentrate it while you are construction on another wizard spell. you make checks for all the spells you are concentrating on and you lose constructing on all the spells on a fail.
