## Artificer Specialist 

### Commando


#### Tool Proficiency
*3rd level Quantum Smith Feature*<br>
You gain proficiency with your choice of smith's tools, carver's tools, or jeweler's tools.  If you already have these proficiencies, you gain proficiency with one other type of artisan's tool of your choice.

#### Commando Spells
*3rd level Quantum Smith Feature*<br>
You always have certain spells prepared after you reach particular levels in this class, as shown in the Quantum Smith Spells table.

#### Arcane Armaments
*3rd level Quantum Smith Feature*<br>
Get some guns.

* At the end of a long rest pick out a number of guns you meet the prerequisite for equal to your proficiency modifier and replenish all your ammo.  Those are the guns you have access to for the day.
* Every gun has a certain ammount of ammo it can fire in a row before it needs to be reloaded, which can be done as an action or in place of an attack as part of an attack action
* As an action, you can expend a spell slot to restore all the missing ammo of a gun.  The minimum required level of the spell slot depends on the gun.

#### Extra Attack
*5th level Quantum Smith Feature*<br>
You can attack twice, rather than once, whenever you take an Attack action on your turn.

#### Custom Munitions
*9th level Quantum Smith Feature*<br>
When you build a gun after completing a long rest, you can apply a modification to it.  The available modifications are listed in the gun's description.

#### Up Your Arsenal 
*15th level Quantum Smith Feature*<br>
When you reload a gun, you can temporarily enhance it.  You gain a bonus to attack rolls with the weapon equal to your Intelligence modifier, the weapon's save DCs are increased by your Intelligence modifier, and when you roll for damage with it, roll twice the number of damage dice.

The effect lasts until you reload the gun again.  You can use this feature a number of times equal to your Intelligence modifier and regain all uses after completing a long rest.

## Guns
These are the guns.  Only you can use them.  You can stow one gun and draw another as part of the same object interaction.  All guns have the following properties:

##### Ammo Count
This is the maximum amount of ammunition that can be carried for the weapon, and the amount it starts with when you first create it.

##### Reload
This weapon can be fired a number of times before it must be reloaded using an action or in place of an attack that's part of an attack action or in place of an off hand attack while duel wielding.  The weapon an be reloaded early without losing any ammunition.

##### Restock
This is the level of the spell slot that must be expended to replenish all the weapon's ammunition.

### Additional Gun Properties

##### Automatic(X)
When you make a ranged attack without disadvantage with this weapon against a creature, you may immediately make up to X extra attacks against it, each with disadvantage.

##### Deadly
This weapon deals an additional damage die on a critical hit and scores a critical hit on a roll of 19 or 20.

##### Point Blank
Being within 5 feet of a hostile creature doesn't imposed disadvantage on ranged attacks made with this weapon.

##### Siege
This weapon deals double damage to objects and structures

##### Spread
This weapon ignores half-cover, treats 3/4 cover as half-cover, and treats creatures as full cover.  It also ignores the damage resistances of swarm creatures.

### The Guns

#### Side Arm
*Simple ranged weapon, 1d6 piercing, range 20/120, ammunition(36)  reload(12), light, point blank*


***Dual Wielding.*** When you create this gun you can create copy of it that draws from the same pool of ammo.  They each need to be reloaded separately, but you can reload them while they are occupying both your hands.

***Silencer.*** Attacks with this weapon don't make any sound and if you are hidden, they don't reveal your location.

***Automatic.*** The weapon gains automatic(1).

***Hand Cannon.*** The weapon's reload changes to reload(1), its damage changes to 2d6 piercing, and it gains the deadly property.

***Pepper Box.*** The weapon gains the spread property

\columnbreak

#### Shot Gun
*Simple ranged weapon, 1d8 piercing, range 15/60, ammunition(36), reload(6), point blank, spread, two-handed*

***Super Spread.*** When you hit this this weapon, creatures within 5 feet of the target of an attack that don't have any cover take 1d4 weapon damage.

***Shielded Stock.*** You have a +1 to AC while wielding this weapon.

***Suppressing Fire.*** Creatures you hit this weapon can't make opportunity attacks against you this turn.

***Elemental Blasts.*** When you reload the weapon, you can change its damage type to your choice of acid, cold, lightning, or thunder.

***Knock Back.***  When you hit a creature within 5 feet of you with the weapon, you can choose to move it 5 feet away from you.

#### Assault Rifle
*Simple ranged weapon, 1d8 piercing, range 100/200, ammunition(36), reload(6), automatic(1), two-handed*

***Bayonet.***  You can use the gun as a melee weapon that deals 1d6 piercing damage.  Attacking this way does not consume ammo and doesn't benefit from the Up Your Arsenal feature.

***Hand Cannon.*** The weapon's reload changes to reload(1), its damage changes to 2d8 piercing, and it gains the deadly property.

***Standing Turret.***  If you haven't moved this turn, you can negate the disadvantage imposed by automatic.  Doing this reduces your movement to 0 for the remainder of your turn.

***Rapid Fire.*** The weapon's automatic trait changes to automatic(2).

***Shielded Stock.*** You have a +1 to AC while wielding this weapon.

#### Sniper Rifle
*Simple ranged weapon, 1d12 piercing, range 200/600, ammunition(12), reload(1), deadly, special, two-handed*<br>
Attacks with this weapon suffer disadvantage and cannot be made beyond its short range.  This can be negated if you haven't moved this turn and forgo any movement for the rest of your turn.  

***Piercing Shot.***  When you hit a creature with this weapon, you can immediately make another attack roll against a creature directly behind your target that doesn't have cover and is within your weapon's range.

***Silencer.*** Attacks with this weapon don't make any sound and if you are hidden, they don't reveal your location.

***Assassin's Tool.*** If hit with an attack that had advantage against a creature that hasn't taken its turn yet, you automatically score a critical hit.

***Slowing Shot.***  A creature hit by this weapon must make a Constitution saving throw against your spell save DC.  On a failure, its move speed is halved until the beginning of your next turn.

***Knock Back Shot.***  A creature hit by this weapon must make a Strength saving throw against your spell save DC. On a failure, it is pushed 5 feet away from you.

\pagebreak

#### Minigun
*Simple ranged weapon, 1d6 piercing, range 60/120, ammunition(200), reload(50), automatic(3), special, two-handed*<br>
As an action, you can expend 10 ammunition to force every creature in a 60 foot cone to make a Dexterity saving throw against your spell save DC.  Creatures more than 30 feet away from you make this save with advantage.  Creatures take 5d6 piercing damage on a failure and half as much on a success.  You cannot use this feature if you are less than 10 shots away from needing to reload.

***Standing Turret.***  If you haven't moved this turn, you can negate the disadvantage imposed by automatic and, when you use the weapon's special trait, creatures no longer gain advantage on their saving throw for being more than 30 feet away.  Doing this reduces your movement to 0 for the remainder of your turn. 

***Shielded Stock.*** You have a +1 to AC while wielding this weapon.

***All Out Attack.*** When you use the weapon's special property, you can choose to expend 20 ammunition and deal 10d6 piercing damage.

***Siege Machine.***  The weapon gains the spread and siege properties.

***Run and Gun.***  At the start of your turn, you can choose to run and gun for the turn.  When you do this, for the rest of your turn you have half movement, you can use the weapon's special cone attack in place of any normal attacks with this weapon, and the cone attack's damage is reduced to 3d6 piercing damage.

#### RPG
*Simple ranged weapon, 1d4 bludgeoning, range 60/100, ammunition(12), reload(1), siege, special, two-handed*<br>
When shot from this weapon hits its target, hits a solid surface, or reaches its maximum range, it explodes outwards in a 10-foot radius sphere.  Each creature in the area must make a dexterity save against your spell save DC or take Xd6 fire damage.  A creature hit by the weapon's automatically fails this save.

***Cruise Missles.***  The weapon's range increases to 100/300

***Elemental Blasts.*** When you reload the weapon, you can change its damage type to your choice of acid, cold, lightning, or thunder.

***Knock Back Blast.***  Creatures that fail their save are pushed 10 feet away from the center of the blast.

***Curving Missles.***  When you miss with an attack with this weapon, you can use a bonus action to to reroll the attack roll against a different target within 60 feet of the original target.

***Seeker Missle.***  When you attack with this weapon, you may choose one creature you have seen in the past minute. The shot flies toward that creature, moving around corners if necessary and ignoring three-quarters cover and half cover. If the target is within the weapon’s range and there is a path large enough for the shot to travel to the target, you can make the weapon's attack against that target. You are able to perceive.

#### Bomb Launcher
*Simple ranged weapon, ammunition(12), reload(1), siege, special*<br>
When you fire this weapon, choose a space within 30 feet of you to launch its shot into.  At the end of your turn, the shot fired explodes outwards in a 20-foot radius sphere. Each creature in the area must make a dexterity save against your spell save DC or take Xd6 fire damage.

***Delayed Blast.***  You can choose not to have the bomb explode at the end of your turn and instead wait to use an object interaction to detonate the explosion on a later turn.  When you do so, all bombs you have shot explode at once.  When you complete a long rest, all bombs you layed automatically diffuse and cease to function.

***Elemental Blasts.*** When you reload the weapon, you can change its damage type to your choice of acid, cold, lightning, or thunder.

***Knock Back Blast.***  Creatures that fail their save are pushed 10 feet away from the center of the blast.

***Rolling Bombs.***  Rather than firing the bomb, you can choose to roll it along a flat surface.  When you attack this way, the bomb's range is doubled.  The bomb can move up walls and even along ceilings, but it cannot turn or change directions.

***Cluster Bomb.***  The explosion from the bomb ignores cover.



#### Elemental Thrower
*Simple ranged weapon, ammunition(30), reload(6), special*<br>
When you create this weapon, choose a damage type from the following: acid, cold, fire, lightning, thunder.  When you fire this weapon, each creature in a 15-foot line in front of you must make a Dexterity saving throw against your spell save DC or take Xd6 + your Intelligence modifier damage of the chosen damage type.

***Suppressing Fire.*** Creatures that failed their saving throw against this weapon can't make opportunity attacks against you this turn.

***Adjustable Nozzle.***  When you fire this weapon, you can choose to change the area of its attack to a 10-foot cone.

***Heavy Thrower.***  The range of the weapon increases to 25 feet and it gains the two-handed property.

***Spinning Spray.***  When you fire this weapon, you can choose to change the area of its attack to every space within 5 feet of you.

***Light Thrower.*** The weapon gains the light property and its range decreases to 10 feet.  Damage from an off-hand attack doesn't apply your Intelligence modifier. 

#### Laser Gun
*Simple ranged weapon, 1d6 radiant, ammunition(30), reload(6), special*<br>
When you fire this weapon, choose a straight line up to 15 feet long within 30 feet of you.  Make an attack roll against each creature of your choice in that line.  If the line is no longer than 5 feet long, you have advantage on these attack rolls.

***Light Weapon.***  The weapon gains the light property, and you may create a second one.  Both weapons draw from the same ammo pool.

***Heavy Weapon.***  The weapon gains the two-handed property and its damage changes to 1d10.

\pagebreak

***Precision Laser.***  The line you draw with the laser no longer needs to be straight.

***Power Ramp.*** The weapon's damage changes to 1d4 radiant, the line it can draw can only be up to 5 feet, and you no longer gain advantage on attacks because you made a 5-foot line.  Every time you fire the weapon, its damage increases by an additional 1d4 radiant damage.  This continues until you spend a turn without making an attack with it, or you reload it.

***Plasma Cutter.***  The weapon gains the siege and spread properties.  

#### Devastator
*Simple ranged weapon, 1d6 fire, range 30/60, ammunition(30), reload(10), automatic(2), siege, spread, special, two-handed*<br>
Whenever you miss with an attack roll from this weapon, you still deal half damage to the target.  Additionally, you may use an action to target a point you can see within 60 feet of you and use 10 ammo to launch of cluster of rockets at the area around that point.  Make an attack roll with this weapon against every creature within 10 feet of that point.

***Elemental Blasts.*** When you reload the weapon, you can change its damage type to your choice of acid, cold, lightning, or thunder.

***Standing Turret.***  If you haven't moved this turn, you can negate the disadvantage imposed by automatic.  Doing this reduces your movement to 0 for the remainder of your turn.

***Super Spread.*** When you hit with this weapon, creatures within 5 feet of the target of an attack that don't have any cover take 1d4 weapon damage.

***Cluster Shots.***  This weapon gains the spread property.

***Scorched Earth.***  If you launch of cluster of rockets at an area, it remains covered in flames until the start of your next turn.  Any creature that enters an area covered in fire for the first time or ends its turn there takes 2d6 fire damage.


#### Energy Blade
*Simple melee weapon, 1d8 force, ammunition(36), reload(4)*<br>

***Self-Regnerating Blade.***  This weapon never outs of ammo and reloading it can be done as an object interaction.

***Energy Smite.***  When you hit with this weapon, you can expend the rest of the ammo you have before you'll need to reload and deal an additional 1d8 force damage to the target per ammo expended this way.

***Light Blade.***  The weapon gains the light property.

***Great Blade.***  The weapon gains the two-handed property, its damage die changes to 1d12, and you gain the benefits of the Great Weapon Master feat while wielding it.

***Thrown Blade.***  The weapon gains the thrown (10/20) property and automatically returns to your hand when thrown.

***Slayer Blade.***  If a strike from this weapon reduces a creature's hit points to less than or equal to your artificer level, you instantly kill it and gain temporary hit points equal to your artificer level.

\columnbreak

#### Ricochet Blade Launcher
*Simple ranged weapon, 1d8 slashing, range 30/60, ammunition(30), reload(6), special*<br>
When you attack with this weapon, you may choose to target a solid surface within your it's range to bounce the shot off of.  When you do so, you may make the weapon attack against a creature from the direction of the ricochet.  
The total distance your shot travels cannot exceed your weapons range, and disadvantage is applied to attacks that exceed its short range, as usual. The ricochet attack is also made with disadvantage if you cannot see the target creature.

***Scout Blades.***  Your shots have darkvision out to 30 feet and you fire them, you can perceive anywhere they've been until the end of your turn.

***Double Bounce.***  The weapon's range increases to 40/80 and its shots can ricochet a second time.

***Victim Bounce.***  If a shot from the weapon hits a creature without having bounced, you can bounce it off that creature to attempt another attack against a target within range.  This attack doesn't apply your ability modifier to its damage roll.

***Rolling Blade.***  Rather than firing the blade, you can choose to have it roll along a flat surface.  When you attack this way, the blade's range is doubled.  The blade can move up walls and even along ceilings, but it cannot turn or change directions.

***Death Blade.*** The weapon's reload changes to reload(1), its damage changes to 2d8 piercing, and it gains the deadly property.
