## Otherworldly Patron

### The Archfey patron
You have made a pact with a fiend from the lower planes of existence, a being whose aims are evil, even if you strive against those aims. Such beings desire the corruption or destruction of all things, ultimately including you. Fiends powerful enough to forge a pact include demon lords such as Demogorgon, Orcus, Fraz'Urb-luu, and Baphomet; archdevils such as Asmodeus, Dispater, Mephistopheles, and Belial; pit fiends and balors that are especially mighty; and ultroloths and other lords of the yugoloths.

#### Expanded Spell List
The Archfey lets you choose from an expanded list of spells when you learn a warlock spell. The following spells are added to the warlock spell list for you.

#### Fey Presence
Starting at 1st level, your patron bestows upon you the ability to project the beguiling and fearsome presence of the fey. As an action, Target creature of your chooses with in 60 feet of you must make Wisdom saving throw against your warlock spell save DC, which it can choose to fail. the creature fails you must immediately use its reaction, if available, to move as far as its speed allows away or towards you. The creature doesâ€™t move into obviously dangerous ground, such as a fire or a pit. You may then have the creature become Charmed or Frightened of you until the end of your next turn. at 10 level you can use this feature on with 120 feet of you.

Once you use this feature, you can't use it again until you finish a long rest or until you expend a warlock spell slot.

#### Misty Escape
Starting at 6th level, you can vanish in a puff of mist in response to harm.  As bonus action or a reaction to when you take damage, you turn invisible and teleport up to 60 feet to an unoccupied space you can see. You remain invisible until the start of your next turn or until you attack or cast a spell.

#### Beguiling Defenses
Beginning at 10th level, your patron teaches you how to turn the mind-affecting magic of your enemies against them. You have advantage on saving throws against being charmed or frightened.

In addition, whenever you or a creature you can see within 120 feet of you succeeds on a saving throw against being charmed or frightened, you can use your reaction to use you Fey Presence on creature with out expending use and even if you have no uses of the feature left.

#### Fabled Passage
At 14th level, As a bonus action you open circular portal to the fey realm, 15 feet in diameter, on solid surface with in 60 feet of you for the next minute. If the portal would open in the space occupied by a creature, the portal does not open.

While the portal is open, Any creature or object entering the portal is transported to and lost in your patterns realm, The target can use its action to attempt to escape. When it does so, it makes a Wisdom (Survival) check. If it succeeds, it escapes and returns to the nearest unoccupied space next to the portal. Once a creature escapes from the realm, it automatically succeeds on any checks to escape form the realm after.

When the portal closes, all creatures and objects are ejected from the portal.

#### Old One Eldritch Invocations.

#### Bow of the Wild Hunt
Prerequisite: The Archfey patron, Pact of the Blade feature.

You can create a short bow using your Pact of the Blade feature. When you draw back its string and fire, it creates an arrow of white wood, which vanishes at the end of your next turn. In addition When you use your Fey Presence feature on a creature, you may make attack against the creature as part of the same action after all effects of your Fey Presence have resolved. If the creature failed the saving throw, you have advantage on the attack roll.

#### Folio of Fancies

Prerequisite: The Archfey patron, Pact of the tome feature.

Whenever you cast expend a warlock spell slot to cast a spell with your Book of Shadows in hand, you may teleport up to 10 feet to an unoccupied space that you can see, you can do this before or after you cast the spell.

When you use Fey Presence feature, you can choose to have the creature move away or towards form your familiar rather then you if your familiar is with in line of sight of the creature. the creature also becomes frightened or Charmed by your familiar as well as you.
