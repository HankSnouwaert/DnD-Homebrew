## Ranger Archetype

### Witherbloom



#### Poison Crafter
Starting at 3rd level, you learn one to create variety of deadly poison to apply to your weapons, 

**Poisons.** You learn three poison recipes of your choice. You learn two additional poison recipes of your choice at 5th, 9th, 13th and 17th level. You can also replace one recipes you know with a different one at the end of each long rest.

**Making Poisons** The Poison table shows how many Poison slots you have to to make poisons of 1st level and higher.  To make one of these Poison you must expend a slot of the Poison's level or higher and spend 1 minute with your poisoner's kit to create a dose of the poison. Which can be applied to one piece of ammunition as action. poison last 8 hours after it creation or until it hits a creature, if attack miss a creature, you can recover the dart with the poison intact.

You regain all expended Poison slots when you complete a long rest.

**Poisons Known** You know three 1st-level poison recipes of your choice from the list at the end of this document.

The poison recipes column of the Demon Soul Surges table shows when you learn more Demonic Surges of 1st level or higher.

Whenever you gain a level in this class, you can replace one of the poison recipes you know with another poison recipes of your choice. The new poison recipes must be of a level for which you have poison slots.

**Saving Throws.** Your poison require your target to make a saving throw to resist the poison's effects. The saving throw DC is calculated as follows:

Maneuver save DC = 8 + your proficiency bonus + your wisdom modifier (your choice)

#### Blowgun Expert
At 3rd level Your Expert at welding Blowgun, you gain the following benefits.
- You ignore the loading quality of blowguns.
- Being within 5 feet of a hostile creature doesn’t impose disadvantage on your ranged attack rolls of your blowgun.
- blowgun counts as a light weapon for you.
- When you use the Attack action and attack with a one handed weapon, you can use a bonus action to attack with a blowgun you are holding.
- You gain proficiency with the poisoner's kit if you don't already have it. With one hour of work using a poisoner's kit and expending 5 gp worth of materials, you can create a 50 poison blowgun ammunition, the damage for ammunition deals a additional 1d6 poison damage. Ammunition retains it potency for 8 hours or until you hit a creature with it.

#### Specialized Toxin
Starting at 7th level, upon completing a long rest, you may chose a creature type to focus your poisons against. Until you complete another long rest, your poisons you create with this class ignores resistance and immune possessed by creatures of that type. You can also apply the poisoned condition to creatures of the chosen type that are normally are immune.

If the poison already normally ignores poisoned condition immunity of the creature type, the creature instead has disadvantage on the saving throws of the poison.

A creature subjected to this poison must succeed on a Constitution saving throw, taking 3d4 poison damage on a failed save and it falls prone and can't take reactions from the pain, or half as much damage on a successful one and the creature does not fall prone and can still take reactions.
