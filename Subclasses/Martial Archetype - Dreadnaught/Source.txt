## Martial Archetype

### Dual Shield Fighting. 
Beginning when you choose this archetype at 3rd level, you gain the ability to use to shields to there full potential, if you have a shield in both hands, you gain a + 1 to AC bonus to one of your shields.
In addition while you are wielding a shield you can makes attacks with it, it counts as a Light Martial Melee Weapon that deals 1d4 bludgeoning damage. if the shield is magical and gives bonus to your ac, then the shield will gain that much bounce to attack and damage rolls made with it and count as magical.


#### Stand Behind
Starting at 3rd level You are able to rush to defence of your allies, when one of your allies that you can see ends there turn or is attacked. You can move up to half your speed as a reaction, You must end this move closer to the to the ally than you started. In addition if the ally was attack, you may use your Interception or Protection fighting style features as part of this reaction as long you with in range to do so.

#### Wall of steel. 
Starting at 7th level, you become a living barricade. At the start of your turn if you are Welding a shield, you may choose to hold it up in one of the fallowing direction, north, south, east, west or above. as long as you are not prone or incapacitated you and your allies with in 5 feet of you and behind your shield have half cover from effects and attacks in that direction, you can't lower or change the direction of the shield until the start of your next turn or when use your stand Stand Behind feature.


\columnbreak

#### Shiled charge
At 10th level, On your turn, you can spend all your movement to move up to your movement speed in a straight line in the direction of your Wall of steel feature. The first creature you hit with a attack made with your shield during this movement that are in the line must also succeed on Strength saving throw (DC 8 + your proficiency bonus + your Strength modifier) or be knocked prone pushed ahead of you for the rest of this move.


#### Tireless Guardian
Starting at 15th level, Your efforts are double in keeping your a allies safe. You gain a additional Reaction, this reaction can only be used for your Stand Behind feature and can't be used on the same turn you use your reaction.

#### Impassible
At 18th level, Your now a wall that few can breach, your Wall of steel feature gives three-quarters cover.
