**Tenet 1.** Be the Juggernaut

**Tenet 2.** Be the Juggernaut

#### Channel Divinity
When you take this oath at 3rd level, you gain the following two Channel Divinity Options

**Storm Bolt.** As an action, or in place of a melee weapon attack, you may use your Channel Divinity to hurl a bolt of elemental energy at a creature you can see within 30 ft. of you.  Make a ranged spell attack against that creature.  On hit, the creature takes X thunder damage and is stunned until the start of your next turn.

**Shock Wave.** An an action, or in place of a melee weapon attack, you may use your Channel Divinity to slam the ground to create a shock wave.  The shock wave may extend out from you in a 20 ft. radius or along a 40 ft. line.  Creatures in the path of the shock wave must make a Strength save, taking X bludgeoning damage on a fail.  On a pass, the creature takes half damage.

#### Aura of Warding
Starting at 7th level, you may use a bonus action enter a defensive state, warding off damage.  While in this state you have the following changes

* Your speed is reduced by X
* Your AC and the AC of all creatures of your choice within 10ft. of you is increased by X.  At 18th level, this range increases to 30 ft.
* Once per turn, when you strike a creature with a melee weapon attack, you can unleash a concussive blast, forcing all creatures within 5 ft. of it make a Constitution save or take X thunder damage

You may leave this state using a bonus action.  

#### Upgrade Channel Divinities
Starting at 15th level, your Channel Divinity options gain the additional benefits.

**Storm Bolt.** When you use Storm Bolt on a target within reach of a melee weapon attack, you may force that creature to make a Strength saving throw.  On a fail, you knock the creature back 30 ft.

**Shock Wave.** When you use Shock Wave you can choose to pull all creatures who fail their save 10ft. towards you.  Alternatively, you may choose to push all creatures that fail their save 10ft. away from you.

#### Stone Avatar
At 20th level, you may use your action to enter a stony, avatar state.  As part of entering this state, you may leap up to 30 ft. into an unoccupied space you can see.  All creatures within 5 ft. of your landing location must make a Constitution saving throw, taking X damage on a fail and and half as much on a pass.

This state lasts for 1 minute and in it, you gain the following benefits:

* You gain all the benefits your defensive state from Aura of Warding without the movement speed reduction

* You gain resistance to slashing, bludgeoning, and piercing damage

* You gain immunity to the stunned condition and your movement speed cannot be reduced by any means

#### Channel Divinity
When you take this oath at 3rd level, you gain the following two Channel Divinity Options.  Both are magical stances that can be entered or exited using a bonus action.  You can spend up to 10 minutes in either of these stances, all at once or in multiple shorter durations, each one using a minimum of 1 minute from the total duration.  After the full duration as been used, you expend your use of Channel Divinity.

**Artillery Stance.** As a bonus action, you may enter a defensive state, warding off damage.  While in this state you have the following changes

* Your speed is reduced by 15 ft.

* When you enter this stance you can summon a magic hammer into into your hand.  The hammer deals 1d10 thunder or radiant damage (your choice) and has the thrown (60/120) property.  The hammer returns to your hand immediately after it is used to make a ranged attack, and you can use your Divine Smite feature as part of ranged attacks made with it. If lost, the hammer can be resummoned using a bonus action.  The hammer disappears when you leave this stance.  

* You may cast your Oath spells that have a casting time of 1 action using a bonus action

**Melee Stance.** As a bonus action, you may enter a defensive state, warding off damage.  While in this state you have the following changes

* Your speed is reduced by 15 ft.

* Your AC is increased by 1

* You may cast *Thunderclap* as a bonus action. It counts as an Oath of the Juggernaut spell for you, and you use Charisma as your spellcasting ability for it.

#### Aura of The Juggernaut
At 7th level, you and creatures within 10ft. of you ignore the movement penalty imposed by difficult terrain and have advantage on checks and saving throws against effects that would knock you prone or move you against your will.  

Additionally, you may use your bonus action to grant yourself, and all allies of your choice within 10ft. of you, temporary hit points equal to X.  Once you use this ability, you cannot do so again until you complete a short rest.

At 18th level, the range of both these abilities increases to 30 ft.

#### Gravitational Pull
Starting at 15th level, your gain extra benefits while in your stances. 

While in artillery stance, when you hit a creature with your magic hammer or a creature fails its save against one of your Oath spells, you may move that creature up to 10 ft. towards or away from you.  

While in melee stance, once per turn, when you strike a creature with a melee attack, you can force it to treat any movement away from you as though it is moving through difficult terrain.

you have advantage on melee attacks you make as part of your reaction.

#### Stone Avatar
At 20th level, you may use your action to enter a stony, avatar state.  As part of entering this state, you may leap up to 30 ft. into an unoccupied space you can see.  All creatures within 5 ft. of your landing location must make a Constitution saving throw, taking X damage on a fail and and half as much on a pass.

This state lasts for 1 minute and in it, you gain the following benefits:

* You are under the effects of the enlarge option from the *Enlage/Reduce* spell

* You have Resistance to bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage.

* You are under the effects of both stances granted by your Channel Divinity feature without suffering a movement speed reduction

* You cannot be stunned or paralyzed and your movement speed cannot be reduced by any means
