## Ranger Archetype

### Steppe Rider


#### Superior Steed
*3rd level Steppe Rider Feature*<br>
You may cast the *find steed* spell once per day without expending a spell slot.  When you cast *find steed* this way, it gains the following benefits:

• Your steed's hit points maximum is increased by 3 x your ranger level

• Your steed is difficult to pin down during a fight.  While you are mounted on the steed, it can use its reaction to move up to 10 feet when a creature you can see ends its turn within 20 feet of you.  This movement doesn't provoke opportunity attacks against you or your steed.

• When you gain your Land's Stride feature at 8th level and Vanish feature at 14th level, your steed also gains these features.

#### Rapid Fire
*3rd level Steppe Rider Feature*<br>
When you take the attack action with a ranged weapon that lacks the heavy property and haven't moved during this turn, you can attack twice instead of once. Your speed then becomes 0 until the end of the turn.

At 5th level, the number of attacks you can make increases to three.

#### High Speed Barrage
*7th level Steppe Rider Feature*<br>
At 7th level, When you take the attack action, if you are at least 25 feet way form any space you made ranged weapon this turn when you make a attack on the same creature with the same weapon, you gain advantage on the attack.

#### Poison Crafter
At 11th level, When a creautre you can see moves twords you and is with in 25 feet of you, you can make one ranged attack aginst that creature.

Being within 5 feet of a hostile creature doesn’t impose disadvantage on your attack rolls with ranged weapons that lack the heavy property.

When a hostile creature's movement provokes an opportunity attack from you, you can make the attack with a ranged weapon.


#### Manipulative Shot
Starting at 15th level, When you hit a creature with three ranged attacks on the same turn, you may have the creature make a wisdome saving throw against your spell save, on a fail you may have the creature be subjected to one of the fallowing effects.

• Until the start of it's next turn, the creature has disadvantage on Attack rolls against you and your Steed while you are within Line of Sight, The the creature can’t willingly move closer to you. A creature that can't be frightened is immune to the effect.

• Until the start of it's next turn, the creature has disadvantage on Attack rolls against creatures other then you and your Steed while you are within Line of Sight, The creature can’t willingly move way form you. A creature that can't be charmed is immune to the effect.
