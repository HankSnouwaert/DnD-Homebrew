> ## Chariot of The Forest
>*Large Chariot, 50 lbs*
> ___
> - **Armor Class** 17
> - **Hit Points**  136 (16d10 + 48)
> - **Damage Immunities** Poison, Psychic 
> - **Damage Vulnerabilities** Fire
> - **Max Speed** 60ft. 
> - **Steed Capacity** 1 Large creature
> - **Carriage Capacity** 1 Medium creature
> - **Constitution Modifier** +3
> - **Challenge** 3 (700 XP)
>___
>
> ***Thicket Runner.*** The chariot and its steeds ignore the effects of non-magical difficult terrain. They can also pass through plants without being slowed, grappled, or restrained and without taking damage from them if they have thorns, spines, or similar hazards.
>
> ***Trample.*** The chariot and its steeds can move through a hostile creature’s space without expending extra movement if that creature is prone and Medium or smaller. If the chariot or its steeds end their turn inside a creature’s space, they are pushed into the nearest unoccupied space.
>
> ***Tree Strider.*** The chariot, its steeds, and all its passengers are under the effects of the *tree stride* spell, and all teleport at once when moving between trees. Both trees used for teleporting must be Large or larger, and the tree being teleported to must have enough unoccupied space adjacent to it to hold the chariot and its steed.
>
> ### Steed Actions
>
> ***Entangling Charge.*** Each steed may make a melee weapon attack against each creature it is facing within 5 feet of it. If the chariot moves at least 20 feet in a straight line before the attacks are made, each attack deals an additional 10 (3d6) damage, and each creature hit must succeed on a DC 13 Strength saving throw or be restrained by vines for the next minute. <br>
> A restrained creature or another creature that can touch it can use its action to make a DC 13 Strength check to attempt to break the restraints, destroying them on a success.
>

> ## Burning Chariot
>*Large Chariot, 60 lbs.*
> ___
> - **Armor Class** 18
> - **Hit Points**  171 (18d10 + 72)
> - **Damage Immunities**  Fire, Poison, Psychic
> - **Max Speed** 50ft. 
> - **Steed Capacity** 2 Large creatures
> - **Carriage Capacity** 4 Medium creatures
> - **Constitution Modifier** +4
> - **Challenge** 4 (1,100 XP)     
> ___
>
> ***Burning Tracks.*** Any ground the burning chariot moves through is set ablaze with fire for the next minute. A creature takes the 10 (3d6) fire damage when it enters the area for the first time on a turn or ends its turn there.
>
> ### Steed Actions
>
> ***Heat Surge.*** If the chariot has moved at least 20 feet in a straight line this turn, its steeds may have flames shoot forward, filling a 10-foot square in front of each steed. Each creature caught in the flames must make a DC 13 Dexterity saving throw, taking 21 (6d6) fire damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. Any ground in the area of the flames is then subjected to the effects of the chariot’s Burning Tracks feature.

> ## Chariot of the Sea
>*Large Chariot, 60 lbs.*
> ___
> - **Armor Class** 18
> - **Hit Points**  114 (12d10+48)
> - **Damage Immunities** Poison, Psychic, Fire
> - **Max Speed** Swim 60ft. 
> - **Steed Capacity** 2 Large creatures
> - **Carriage Capacity** 2 Medium creatures
> - **Constitution Modifier** +3
> - **Challenge** 1 (700 XP)     
> ___
>
>
> ### Steed Actions
>
> ***Tile Surge*** Chariot 60 may move up to swim speed though a body of water in a straight line. While the Chariot is moving, each creature 10 or less feet adjacent to the Chariot as it moves past must make a DC 13 strength saving throw or be knocked prone and pushed 10 feet away from the Chariot.

> ## Winged Chariot
>*Large Chariot, 60 lbs.*
> ___
> - **Armor Class** 17
> - **Hit Points**  90 (12d10+24)
> - **Damage Immunities** Poison, Psychic 
> - **Max Speed** 60ft., Flying 60ft. 
> - **Steed Capacity** 2 Large creatures
> - **Carriage Capacity** 2 Medium creatures
> - **Constitution Modifier** +2
> - **Challenge** 1 (50 XP)     
> ___
>
> ### Steed Actions
>
> ***Dive Attack.*** Each steed may make a melee weapon attack against each creature under it within 5 feet of it. If the Chariot is flying and dives at least 30 feet straight line before the attacks are made, each attack deals an additional 10 (3d6) damage, and a creature hit by one or more of the attacks must succeed on a DC 12 Strength saving throw or be knocked prone.
